# Reuters

## Overview
Reuters, a division of Thomson Reuters, is a global leader in news and financial [market](../m/market.md) data. Known for its comprehensive and timely coverage, Reuters provides news, [market](../m/market.md) data, and analysis to financial professionals, businesses, and media organizations worldwide. The platform delivers real-time information across various [asset](../a/asset.md) classes, including equities, commodities, forex, and [fixed income](../f/fixed_income.md).

## Features
1. **Real-Time News**: Provides breaking news, in-depth analysis, and [market](../m/market.md)-moving events from around the world.
2. **[Market](../m/market.md) Data**: Comprehensive [market](../m/market.md) data covering equities, commodities, forex, and [fixed income](../f/fixed_income.md).
3. **[Financial Analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md)**: Tools for [financial analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md), including fundamental data, [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md), and [market sentiment](../m/market_sentiment.md).
4. **Custom Alerts**: Customizable alerts for news, price movements, and [market](../m/market.md) events.
5. **[Data Visualization](../d/data_visualization.md)**: Advanced [data visualization](../d/data_visualization.md) tools for charting and analyzing [market](../m/market.md) trends.
6. **Integration with Trading Platforms**: Seamless integration with trading platforms for real-time data and news.
7. **Research Reports**: Access to detailed research reports and insights from analysts.
8. **[Economic Indicators](../e/economic_indicators.md)**: Real-time data on [economic indicators](../e/economic_indicators.md) and macroeconomic trends.
9. **Mobile Access**: Mobile applications for accessing news, [market](../m/market.md) data, and analysis on the go.
10. **APIs**: Developer-friendly APIs for integrating Reuters data into custom applications and financial systems.

## Key Components
- **Reuters News**: Real-time news service providing comprehensive coverage of global events and [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md).
- **Reuters [Market](../m/market.md) Data**: Real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [asset](../a/asset.md) classes.
- **Reuters [Financial Analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md)**: Tools for conducting in-depth [financial analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md) and research.
- **Data Feeds and APIs**: [Robust](../r/robust.md) data feeds and APIs for integrating Reuters content into trading platforms and applications.
- **Economic Data**: Access to [economic indicators](../e/economic_indicators.md) and macroeconomic data for [market](../m/market.md) analysis.

## Integrations
Reuters integrates with a variety of financial platforms, data providers, and third-party applications to enhance its functionality. Some notable integrations include:

- **Trading Platforms**: Compatibility with popular trading platforms such as [Bloomberg](../b/bloomberg.md), MetaTrader, and Reuters Eikon for seamless [data integration](../d/data_integration.md) and [execution](../e/execution.md) capabilities.
- **[Financial Analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md) Tools**: Integration with tools like Microsoft Excel, MATLAB, and R for advanced analysis and visualization.
- **APIs**: Provides APIs for custom integrations and automated data retrieval.
- **Third-Party Applications**: Support for various third-party financial applications and services.

## Community and Support
Reuters offers extensive support through detailed documentation, user guides, tutorials, and a dedicated support team. Users can also access a [range](../r/range.md) of educational resources, including webinars, case studies, and community forums to share insights, ask questions, and receive assistance from other financial professionals and Reuters experts.

- **Individual Investors**: Assists individual investors in staying informed about [market](../m/market.md) developments and making data-driven investment decisions.
