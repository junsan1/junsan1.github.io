# Robinhood

## Overview
Robinhood is a popular online brokerage known for [offering](../o/offering.md) [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading of [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), and cryptocurrencies. The platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible, catering primarily to individual investors and new traders. Robinhood's mission is to democratize [finance](../f/finance.md) for all by providing access to [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md) through an intuitive mobile and web platform.

## Features
1. **[Commission](../c/commission.md)-Free Trading**: [Trade](../t/trade.md) [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), and cryptocurrencies with no [commission](../c/commission.md) fees.
2. **User-Friendly Platform**: Intuitive mobile and web platforms with a simple, easy-to-navigate interface.
3. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), and cryptocurrencies.
4. **[Real-Time Market Data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Access to real-time quotes, charts, and [market](../m/market.md) data.
5. **Fractional [Shares](../s/shares.md)**: Ability to buy fractional [shares](../s/shares.md) of [stocks](../s/stock.md), making it easier to invest in high-priced [stocks](../s/stock.md).
6. **[Cash Management](../c/cash_management.md)**: Features like [cash management](../c/cash_management.md) accounts with competitive [interest](../i/interest.md) rates.
7. **Educational Resources**: Educational materials, including articles, tutorials, and in-app tips to help new investors learn about trading and [investing](../i/investing.md).
8. **Automated [Investing](../i/investing.md)**: Provides [options](../o/options.md) for recurring investments and automatic [dividend reinvestment](../d/dividend_reinvestment.md).
9. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile apps for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
10. **Secure and Regulated**: Robinhood is regulated by top-tier financial authorities, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).

## Key Components
- **Robinhood App**: A user-friendly mobile app [offering](../o/offering.md) [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading, real-time data, and investment tools.
- **Robinhood Web**: A web platform that provides a similar trading experience as the mobile app, with easy access to account information and trading tools.
- **Fractional [Shares](../s/shares.md)**: Allows users to invest in high-priced [stocks](../s/stock.md) by buying fractional [shares](../s/shares.md).
- **[Cash Management](../c/cash_management.md)**: Features that include competitive [interest](../i/interest.md) rates on uninvested cash and a [debit](../d/debit.md) card for spending.
- **Educational Resources**: In-app educational content, articles, and tutorials to help users understand trading and [investing](../i/investing.md).
- **Real-Time Data**: Access to real-time quotes, [market](../m/market.md) data, and customizable watchlists.

## Integrations
Robinhood integrates with a variety of financial platforms and tools to enhance its trading services. Some notable integrations include:

- **Plaid**: Integration with Plaid to securely link [bank](../b/bank.md) accounts for easy deposits and withdrawals.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Data Providers**: Access to real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data.
- **Third-Party Research**: Access to research and analysis tools through partnerships with other financial data providers.
- **APIs**: Provides APIs for custom integrations and automated trading solutions.

## Use Cases
- **Individual Investors**: Utilized by individual investors for [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading and managing their investment portfolios.
- **New Traders**: Supports new traders with educational resources and an easy-to-use platform.
- **Mobile Users**: Enables mobile users to manage their trading activities efficiently through the Robinhood mobile app.
- **Cost-Conscious Investors**: Appeals to investors looking to minimize [trading costs](../t/trading_costs.md) with [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading.
- **[Fractional Share](../f/fractional_share.md) Investors**: Allows investors to buy fractional [shares](../s/shares.md), making it easier to invest in high-priced [stocks](../s/stock.md).

## Regulation
Robinhood is regulated by several top-tier financial authorities, ensuring a secure and compliant [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md):
- Securities and [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) [Commission](../c/commission.md) (SEC)
- Financial [Industry](../i/industry.md) Regulatory Authority ([FINRA](../f/finra.md))
- Securities [Investor](../i/investor.md) Protection [Corporation](../c/corporation.md) (SIPC)
