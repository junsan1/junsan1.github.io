# Zerodha

## Overview
Zerodha is India's largest retail stockbroker, offering a wide range of financial services including [equity trading](../e/equity_trading.md), [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md) trading, commodities, mutual funds, and bonds. Known for its discount brokerage model, advanced trading platforms, and comprehensive educational resources, Zerodha caters to individual investors, traders, and institutions.

## Features
1. **Wide Range of Instruments**: Access to equities, [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md), commodities, mutual funds, and bonds.
2. **Discount Brokerage**: Low-cost trading with a flat fee per trade, making it one of the most cost-effective brokers in India.
3. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers multiple trading platforms including Kite, Coin, Console, and Kite Mobile with advanced charting tools and real-time data.
4. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including webinars, tutorials, articles, and market analysis through Zerodha Varsity.
5. **Market Research and Analysis**: Access to comprehensive research reports, market news, and advanced analysis tools.
6. **API Access**: Robust APIs for automated trading, custom integrations, and developing third-party trading applications.
7. **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md)**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
8. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile apps for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
9. **Mutual Fund Investments**: Platform for investing in direct mutual funds without any commission.
10. **Secure and Regulated**: Zerodha is regulated by top-tier financial authorities in India, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).

## Key Components
- **Kite**: A powerful trading platform offering advanced charting, real-time data, and a range of trading tools.
- **Kite Mobile**: Mobile application for trading, account management, and market research on the go.
- **Coin**: A platform for investing in direct mutual funds.
- **Console**: A back-office platform for tracking investments and generating reports.
- **Educational Resources (Varsity)**: A library of educational content, including webinars, tutorials, and articles.
- **API Access**: APIs for automated trading and custom integrations.

## Integrations
Zerodha integrates with a variety of financial platforms and tools to enhance its trading services. Some notable integrations include:

- **Market Data Providers**: Integration with leading market data providers for real-time and historical data.
- **Third-Party Platforms**: Integration with third-party trading platforms and tools.
- **API Access**: Provides APIs for custom integrations and automated trading solutions.
- **Research Tools**: Access to third-party research and analysis tools.

## Use Cases
- **Individual Investors**: Utilized by individual investors for managing their investments, accessing research, and trading in various markets.
- **Active Traders**: Provides active traders with powerful trading tools, [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md), and advanced analysis.
- **New Traders**: Supports new traders with educational resources and a user-friendly platform.
- **Cost-Conscious Investors**: Appeals to investors looking to minimize [trading costs](../t/trading_costs.md) with a discount brokerage model.
- **Algorithmic Traders**: Supports algorithmic traders in developing and deploying custom [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) through APIs and trading platforms.
- **Portfolio Managers**: Assists portfolio managers in tracking and managing their investment portfolios with comprehensive tools and reports.

## Regulation
Zerodha is regulated by several top-tier financial authorities in India, ensuring a secure and compliant [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md):
- Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI)
- National Stock Exchange (NSE)
- Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE)
- Multi Commodity Exchange (MCX)
