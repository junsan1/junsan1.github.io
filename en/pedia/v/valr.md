# VALR

VALR is a South African-based cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) that has quickly gained traction in the rapidly evolving digital assets [market](../m/market.md). Launched in June 2019, VALR aims to provide a seamless and efficient [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) for both beginners and seasoned traders. The company has carved out a niche for itself by focusing on user experience, [robust](../r/robust.md) [security](../s/security.md) measures, and an extensive [range](../r/range.md) of supported cryptocurrencies.

## Company Overview

VALR was founded by Farzam Ehsani, a former executive at Rand [Merchant Bank](../m/merchant_bank.md), who brought together a team of experts well-versed in [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md) and technology. The company is headquartered in Johannesburg, South Africa, and operates globally with a focus on emerging markets.

### Key Features of VALR:

1. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Cryptocurrencies**: VALR supports over 60 cryptocurrencies, including popular ones like [Bitcoin](../b/bitcoin.md) (BTC), [Ethereum](../e/ethereum_.md) (ETH), and Litecoin (LTC), as well as a host of altcoins.
   
2. **User-Friendly Interface**: The platform is designed to be intuitive, catering to both novice and experienced traders. It includes features like advanced charting tools, [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md), and easy navigation.
   
3. **[Security](../s/security.md)**: VALR employs [industry](../i/industry.md)-standard [security](../s/security.md) measures like two-[factor](../f/factor.md) authentication (2FA), cold storage for the majority of assets, and regular [security](../s/security.md) audits to ensure the safety of users' funds.
   
4. **Competitive Fees**: The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) offers competitive trading fees, which are often lower compared to other platforms. There is also a tiered [fee](../f/fee.md) structure that rewards higher trading volumes with even lower fees.

5. **[Customer](../c/customer.md) Support**: VALR provides 24/7 [customer](../c/customer.md) support through various channels, including email and live chat, ensuring that users can get assistance whenever they need it.

## Trading on VALR

### Account Creation and Verification

Creating an account on VALR is a straightforward process:

1. **Sign-Up**: Users sign up using their email address and create a secure password.
   
2. **Verification**: To comply with regulatory standards, VALR requires users to complete a Know Your [Customer](../c/customer.md) (KYC) process. This involves providing personal information and uploading identification documents.

3. **Two-[Factor](../f/factor.md) Authentication**: For added [security](../s/security.md), users are encouraged to enable 2FA.

### Deposit and Withdrawal

VALR offers [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [options](../o/options.md) for depositing and withdrawing funds:

- **Fiat Currencies**: Users can [deposit](../d/deposit.md) and withdraw South African Rand (ZAR) via [bank](../b/bank.md) transfer. The platform supports instant deposits through integrated banking partners.
  
- **Cryptocurrencies**: Users can [deposit](../d/deposit.md) and withdraw a wide [range](../r/range.md) of cryptocurrencies. Deposits are usually processed instantly, while withdrawals may take some time due to network confirmations.

### Trading Pairs

VALR supports a diverse selection of trading pairs, categorized into:

- **[BTC Markets](../b/btcmarkets.md)**: Pairs involving [Bitcoin](../b/bitcoin.md) (BTC) as the base [currency](../c/currency.md).
- **ETH Markets**: Pairs involving [Ethereum](../e/ethereum_.md) (ETH) as the base [currency](../c/currency.md).
- **ZAR Markets**: Pairs involving South African Rand (ZAR) as the base [currency](../c/currency.md).
- **Stablecoin Markets**: Pairs involving stablecoins like USDC and USDT.

### Advanced Trading Tools

For experienced traders, VALR offers advanced trading tools and features:

- **Spot Trading**: Users can buy and sell cryptocurrencies at current [market](../m/market.md) prices or set limit orders to execute trades at specific price points.
  
- **OTC Trading**: For large-[volume](../v/volume.md) trades, VALR provides an Over-The-Counter (OTC) desk. This enables traders to execute large orders without impacting the [market price](../m/market_price.md).
  
- **API Access**: VALR offers an API for [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) and integration with third-party trading bots. The API provides [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md), [order management](../o/order_management_in_trading.md), and account details.

## Security Protocols

Given the importance of [security](../s/security.md) in the cryptocurrency space, VALR has implemented several layers of protection:

1. **Cold Storage**: The majority of user funds are stored in cold wallets, which are offline and resistant to hacking attempts.
   
2. **Two-[Factor](../f/factor.md) Authentication (2FA)**: Users are encouraged to enable 2FA, which adds an extra layer of [security](../s/security.md) by requiring a second verification step during login.
   
3. **Regular Audits**: The platform undergoes regular [security](../s/security.md) audits by third-party experts to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities.
   
4. **Encryption**: All sensitive data on VALR is encrypted, ensuring that user information is secure from unauthorized access.

## Regulatory Compliance

VALR is committed to maintaining compliance with local and international regulations:

- **KYC and AML**: The platform adheres to Know Your [Customer](../c/customer.md) (KYC) and Anti-[Money Laundering](../m/money_laundering.md) (AML) regulations. This includes verifying user identities and monitoring transactions for suspicious activity.

- **Reporting**: VALR complies with reporting requirements set by regulatory bodies, ensuring [transparency](../t/transparency.md) and accountability.
