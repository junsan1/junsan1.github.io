# Vienna Stock Exchange (WBAG)

The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md), officially known as Wiener Börse AG (WBAG), is the primary [exchange](../e/exchange.md) for trading securities in Austria. Established in 1771, it has a rich history and plays a crucial role in the Austrian [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md). The WBAG offers a variety of financial products, including equities, bonds, and [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md), and it serves as a hub for international investors interested in Central and Eastern Europe.

## Historical Overview

### Establishment and Early History
The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) was founded by Empress Maria Theresa of Austria in 1771, making it one of the oldest stock exchanges in the world. Initially, the [exchange](../e/exchange.md) traded mainly in bonds and [foreign exchange](../f/foreign_exchange.md). 

### 19th and 20th Century Developments
Throughout the 19th century, the [exchange](../e/exchange.md) expanded its operations to include a wider array of securities, such as equities. The 20th century saw further modernization, including the introduction of electronic [trading systems](../t/trading_systems.md).

### Modern Era
In the modern era, the Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) has become a key financial center in Central and Eastern Europe. It has established strong connections with other global [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md) and continually updates its technological [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md) to meet the demands of contemporary trading.

## Key Products and Services

### Equities
The [primary market](../p/primary_market.md) for equities on the Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) includes well-known Austrian companies such as OMV, Erste Group, and Voestalpine. The WBAG offers a [robust](../r/robust.md) platform for the listing and trading of [shares](../s/shares.md).

### Bonds
The WBAG provides a comprehensive [bond market](../b/bond_market.md) where both government and corporate bonds are traded. These include Austrian government bonds, which are popular among international investors for their stability.

### Derivatives
The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) also offers a [range](../r/range.md) of [derivative](../d/derivative.md) products, including [options](../o/options.md) and [futures](../f/futures.md). These products allow investors to [hedge](../h/hedge.md) risks and optimize their investment strategies.

### Indices
The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) maintains several indices that measure the performance of various segments of the [market](../m/market.md). The most prominent of these is the ATX (Austrian Traded [Index](../i/index.md)), which is a [benchmark](../b/benchmark.md) [index](../i/index.md) of the top 20 blue-chip [stocks](../s/stock.md) [listed](../l/listed.md) on the [exchange](../e/exchange.md).

## Market Structure

### Trading Hours
The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) operates on Central European Time (CET), with trading commencing at 9:00 AM and closing at 5:30 PM. These hours align with other major European markets, facilitating [cross-border trading](../c/cross-border_trading.md).

### Market Segments
The WBAG is divided into several [market](../m/market.md) segments, each catering to different types of securities and [investor](../i/investor.md) needs. These include:
- **Prime [Market](../m/market.md)**: For blue-chip [stocks](../s/stock.md) that meet stringent regulatory and reporting requirements.
- **Standard [Market](../m/market.md)**: For mid-sized companies with less stringent requirements.
- **Third [Market](../m/market.md)**: An unregulated [market](../m/market.md) catering to smaller companies and niche products.

## Technology and Innovation

### Xetra Trading System
The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) uses the [Xetra](../x/xetra.md) electronic [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md), which is also used by the [Deutsche Börse](../d/deutsche_börse.md). [Xetra](../x/xetra.md) provides high [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), low latency, and efficient [order](../o/order.md) matching, making it ideal for modern trading needs.

### Data Services
The WBAG offers a [range](../r/range.md) of data services, including [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md), historical data, and analytics. These services are essential for [market](../m/market.md) participants who rely on accurate and timely information to make informed trading decisions.

### Algo Trading
[Algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) is supported on the Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md), facilitated by the [Xetra](../x/xetra.md) platform's high-speed connectivity and advanced [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md). [Market](../m/market.md) participants can deploy various [trading algorithms](../t/trading_algorithms.md) to [capitalize](../c/capitalize.md) on [market](../m/market.md) inefficiencies and automate their [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).

## International Collaboration

### CECC
The Central European [Clearing](../c/clearing.md) and Custody (CECC) is a [joint](../j/joint.md) initiative between the Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) and other regional exchanges. This collaboration aims to improve the [efficiency](../e/efficiency.md) and [security](../s/security.md) of post-[trade](../t/trade.md) processes in the region.

### FESE Membership
The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) is a member of the Federation of European Securities Exchanges (FESE), which allows it to collaborate with other European exchanges and influence regulatory policies at the European level.

## Regulation and Compliance

### Financial Market Authority (FMA)
The Financial [Market](../m/market.md) Authority (FMA) is the primary regulatory body overseeing the Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md). The FMA ensures that the WBAG operates in a fair, transparent, and efficient manner.

### MiFID II Compliance
The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) complies with the Markets in Financial Instruments Directive II ([MiFID II](../m/mifid_ii.md)), which sets out comprehensive regulatory requirements for [market](../m/market.md) [transparency](../t/transparency.md), [investor](../i/investor.md) protection, and trading activities.

## Investments and IPOs

### Initial Public Offerings (IPOs)
The WBAG is a popular venue for companies looking to go public. The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) offers a well-regulated environment with strong [investor](../i/investor.md) [interest](../i/interest.md), making it an attractive option for IPOs.

### International Investments
The Vienna Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) attracts significant [foreign investment](../f/foreign_investment.md), particularly in its blue-chip [stocks](../s/stock.md) and government bonds. The [exchange](../e/exchange.md)'s strategic location and [robust](../r/robust.md) regulatory framework make it a go-to destination for international investors interested in the Central and Eastern European markets.
