# MetaStock

## Overview
MetaStock is a leading software provider specializing in technical analysis and charting software for traders and investors in the financial markets. Developed by Innovative Market Analysis, MetaStock offers a comprehensive suite of tools for analyzing securities, developing trading strategies, and conducting market research. With over three decades of experience, MetaStock serves both retail and institutional clients, providing advanced technology and robust data services to support their trading activities.

## Key Features
1. **Advanced Charting**: MetaStock provides powerful charting capabilities with a wide range of technical indicators, drawing tools, and customization options. Users can analyze price movements, identify trends, and make informed trading decisions.

2. **Technical Analysis Tools**: The software includes hundreds of built-in technical indicators and studies, such as moving averages, Bollinger Bands, MACD, and more. Users can also create their own custom indicators and trading systems.

3. **Expert Advisors**: MetaStock offers Expert Advisors that provide buy and sell signals based on predefined criteria. These advisors can help traders identify potential trading opportunities and make decisions based on technical analysis.

4. **System Testing and Optimization**: Traders can backtest their trading strategies using historical data to evaluate performance and optimize their systems. MetaStock’s system testing capabilities allow users to refine their strategies for better results.

5. **Real-Time Data and News**: MetaStock provides access to real-time market data and news from various exchanges and data providers. This ensures that traders have up-to-date information to make timely trading decisions.

6. **Market Scanning**: The software includes scanning tools that allow users to filter and identify securities that meet specific criteria. This helps traders quickly find trading opportunities based on their strategies.

7. **MetaStock R/T**: For professional traders, MetaStock offers MetaStock R/T, a real-time version of the software that includes additional features and capabilities for active trading.

8. **Integration with Data Providers**: MetaStock integrates with various data providers, including Reuters, allowing users to access a wide range of financial data and news.

9. **Educational Resources**: MetaStock provides extensive educational resources, including webinars, tutorials, and training materials, to help users maximize the potential of the software.
