# MetaStock

## Overview
MetaStock is a leading software provider specializing in [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md) and charting software for traders and investors in the [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md). Developed by Innovative [Market](../m/market.md) Analysis, MetaStock offers a comprehensive suite of tools for analyzing securities, developing [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md), and conducting [market research](../m/market_research.md). With over three decades of experience, MetaStock serves both retail and institutional clients, providing advanced technology and [robust](../r/robust.md) data services to support their trading activities.

## Key Features
1. **Advanced Charting**: MetaStock provides powerful charting capabilities with a wide [range](../r/range.md) of [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md), drawing tools, and customization [options](../o/options.md). Users can analyze price movements, identify trends, and make informed trading decisions.

2. **[Technical Analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md) Tools**: The software includes hundreds of built-in [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md) and studies, such as moving averages, [Bollinger Bands](../b/bollinger_bands.md), MACD, and more. Users can also create their own custom indicators and [trading systems](../t/trading_systems.md).

3. **Expert Advisors**: MetaStock offers Expert Advisors that provide buy and sell signals based on predefined criteria. These advisors can help traders identify potential trading opportunities and make decisions based on [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md).

4. **System Testing and [Optimization](../o/optimization.md)**: Traders can backtest their [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) using historical data to evaluate performance and optimize their systems. MetaStock’s system testing capabilities allow users to refine their strategies for better results.

5. **Real-Time Data and News**: MetaStock provides access to [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md) and news from various exchanges and data providers. This ensures that traders have up-to-date information to make timely trading decisions.

6. **[Market](../m/market.md) Scanning**: The software includes scanning tools that allow users to filter and identify securities that meet specific criteria. This helps traders quickly find trading opportunities based on their strategies.

7. **MetaStock R/T**: For professional traders, MetaStock offers MetaStock R/T, a real-time version of the software that includes additional features and capabilities for active trading.

8. **Integration with Data Providers**: MetaStock integrates with various data providers, including [Reuters](../r/reuters.md), allowing users to access a wide [range](../r/range.md) of financial data and news.

9. **Educational Resources**: MetaStock provides extensive educational resources, including webinars, tutorials, and training materials, to help users maximize the potential of the software.
