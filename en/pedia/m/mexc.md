# MEXC

MEXC (formerly known as MXC [Exchange](../e/exchange.md)) is a cryptocurrency [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) that offers a [range](../r/range.md) of digital [asset](../a/asset.md) trading services to users around the globe. Founded in 2018, MEXC has quickly risen to prominence in the cryptocurrency trading community, due in part to its comprehensive suite of trading features, user-friendly interface, and [robust](../r/robust.md) [security](../s/security.md) measures.

## Overview

### Company Information

MEXC is headquartered in Singapore and operates under MXC Foundation Pte Ltd. The company serves millions of users across 70+ countries and regions. MEXC [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) is designed to [offer](../o/offer.md) a highly secure, efficient, and high-performance trading engine for both novice and seasoned traders.

- **Website**: [mexc.com](https://www.mexc.com)

### Trading Pairs and Assets

MEXC supports a multitude of trading pairs and offers a [wide variety](../w/wide_variety.md) of digital assets. These assets include leading cryptocurrencies like [Bitcoin](../b/bitcoin.md) (BTC), [Ethereum](../e/ethereum_.md) (ETH), and an extensive [range](../r/range.md) of altcoins. Additionally, MEXC frequently adds new tokens, allowing traders to diversify their portfolios and take advantage of emerging digital currencies.

### Security Measures

[Security](../s/security.md) is a top priority at MEXC. The platform incorporates [multiple](../m/multiple.md) layers of protection to shield user funds and data. Key [security](../s/security.md) features include:

- **Cold Wallet Storage**: The majority of user funds are held in cold wallets, isolated from the online network to prevent hacking attempts.
- **Two-[Factor](../f/factor.md) Authentication (2FA)**: Users are encouraged to activate 2FA for an added layer of [security](../s/security.md) during login and transactions.
- **Advanced Encryption**: All data transmissions are encrypted using state-of-the-art encryption protocols.
- **Regular [Security](../s/security.md) Audits**: MEXC conducts frequent [security](../s/security.md) audits to identify and fix vulnerabilities in the system.

## Trading Features

### Spot Trading

Spot trading is one of MEXCâ€™s core offerings. It allows users to buy and sell cryptocurrencies at current [market](../m/market.md) prices. The platform provides a user-friendly interface for spot trading, with features such as:

- **Advanced Charting Tools**: Traders have access to detailed [technical analysis tools](../t/technical_analysis_tools.md) and real-time price charts.
- **[Order Types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md)**: MEXC supports various [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md) including [market](../m/market.md) orders, limit orders, and [stop-limit orders](../s/stop-limit_orders.md).
- **Deep [Liquidity](../l/liquidity.md)**: High trading volumes and deep [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) ensure that trades are executed swiftly with minimal [slippage](../s/slippage.md).

### Margin Trading

For users seeking to amplify their trading potential, MEXC offers [margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md) with [leverage](../l/leverage.md) [options](../o/options.md). [Margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md) allows traders to borrow funds to increase their trading position, with [leverage ratios](../l/leverage_ratios.md) of up to 10x. This feature can enhance profits but also comes with increased [risk](../r/risk.md).

### Futures Trading

MEXC also provides [futures](../f/futures.md) trading, allowing users to [trade](../t/trade.md) cryptocurrency contracts that speculate on the future [value](../v/value.md) of an [asset](../a/asset.md). Key features of MEXC [futures](../f/futures.md) trading include:

- **Perpetual Contracts**: Traders can engage in perpetual contracts which do not have an [expiration date](../e/expiration_date.md).
- **[Leverage](../l/leverage.md)**: High [leverage](../l/leverage.md) [options](../o/options.md) of up to 125x enable traders to maximize potential returns.
- **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md) Tools**: MEXC offers various [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) features such as stop-loss and take-[profit](../p/profit.md) orders to help traders manage their exposure.

### Staking and Earning

MEXC offers staking services where users can lock up their cryptocurrencies to earn rewards over time. Staking on MEXC provides a [passive income](../p/passive_income.md) stream and supports network [security](../s/security.md) and operations. Additionally, MEXC has an "Earn" section that offers various earning products such as savings and [yield](../y/yield.md) farming.

## Fees

MEXC has a competitive [fee](../f/fee.md) structure designed to be accessible to both retail and institutional traders. 

- **Spot Trading Fees**: The platform charges a flat [fee](../f/fee.md) of 0.2% per [trade](../t/trade.md), which can be reduced further through the use of the native MX Token.
- **[Futures](../f/futures.md) Trading Fees**: Fees for [futures](../f/futures.md) trading vary depending on the contract type and [leverage](../l/leverage.md) used. Generally, maker fees can be as low as 0.02% and taker fees around 0.06%.
- **[Deposit](../d/deposit.md) and [Withdrawal](../w/withdrawal.md) Fees**: MEXC does not charge fees for cryptocurrency deposits. [Withdrawal](../w/withdrawal.md) fees vary by [asset](../a/asset.md) and are subject to [blockchain](../b/blockchain_in_trading.md) [transaction costs](../t/transaction_costs.md).

## Token Information: MX Token

The MX Token (MX) is the native [utility](../u/utility.md) token of the MEXC platform. It offers several benefits to users within the ecosystem, including reduced trading fees, voting rights for new token listings, and access to exclusive promotions and events. MX Tokens can also be staked within the MEXC platform to earn additional rewards.
