# MEXC

MEXC (formerly known as MXC Exchange) is a cryptocurrency trading platform that offers a range of digital asset trading services to users around the globe. Founded in 2018, MEXC has quickly risen to prominence in the cryptocurrency trading community, due in part to its comprehensive suite of trading features, user-friendly interface, and robust security measures.

## Overview

### Company Information

MEXC is headquartered in Singapore and operates under MXC Foundation Pte Ltd. The company serves millions of users across 70+ countries and regions. MEXC Exchange is designed to offer a highly secure, efficient, and high-performance trading engine for both novice and seasoned traders.

- **Website**: [mexc.com](https://www.mexc.com)

### Trading Pairs and Assets

MEXC supports a multitude of trading pairs and offers a wide variety of digital assets. These assets include leading cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and an extensive range of altcoins. Additionally, MEXC frequently adds new tokens, allowing traders to diversify their portfolios and take advantage of emerging digital currencies.

### Security Measures

Security is a top priority at MEXC. The platform incorporates multiple layers of protection to shield user funds and data. Key security features include:

- **Cold Wallet Storage**: The majority of user funds are held in cold wallets, isolated from the online network to prevent hacking attempts.
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**: Users are encouraged to activate 2FA for an added layer of security during login and transactions.
- **Advanced Encryption**: All data transmissions are encrypted using state-of-the-art encryption protocols.
- **Regular Security Audits**: MEXC conducts frequent security audits to identify and fix vulnerabilities in the system.

MEXC also provides futures trading, allowing users to trade cryptocurrency contracts that speculate on the future value of an asset. Key features of MEXC futures trading include:

- **Perpetual Contracts**: Traders can engage in perpetual contracts which do not have an expiration date.
- **Leverage**: High leverage options of up to 125x enable traders to maximize potential returns.

Additionally, MEXC has an "Earn" section that offers various earning products such as savings and yield farming.

## Fees

MEXC has a competitive fee structure designed to be accessible to both retail and institutional traders. 

- **Spot Trading Fees**: The platform charges a flat fee of 0.2% per trade, which can be reduced further through the use of the native MX Token.
- **Futures Trading Fees**: Fees for futures trading vary depending on the contract type and leverage used. Generally, maker fees can be as low as 0.02% and taker fees around 0.06%.
- **Deposit and Withdrawal Fees**: MEXC does not charge fees for cryptocurrency deposits. Withdrawal fees vary by asset and are subject to blockchain transaction costs.
