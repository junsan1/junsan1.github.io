# Marketcetera

## Overview
Marketcetera is an open-source trading platform designed to offer a flexible and scalable solution for developing and deploying automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md). It caters to both individual traders and institutional clients by providing a robust framework that supports multiple asset classes and integrates seamlessly with various data providers and execution venues.

## Features
1. **Open-Source Platform**: Offers the flexibility to customize and extend the platform according to specific trading needs.
2. **Automated Trading**: Supports the development and deployment of automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
3. **Multi-Asset Class Support**: Capable of handling equities, options, futures, forex, and other asset classes.
4. **Modular Architecture**: Features a modular architecture that allows for easy integration and customization.
5. **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md)**: Includes tools for managing and monitoring trading risks in real-time.
6. **[Real-Time Market Data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Integration with various data providers to offer [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md).
7. **[Backtesting](../b/backtesting.md)**: Comprehensive [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) capabilities to evaluate [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) against historical data.
8. **Order Management**: Advanced order management system for efficient execution and tracking of trades.
9. **Extensible**: Easily extendable with APIs and SDKs for custom development.

## Key Components
- **Marketcetera Platform**: The core trading platform that provides the infrastructure for developing and executing [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
- **Strategy Engine**: A powerful engine for running automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
- **[Data Integration](../d/data_integration.md)**: Connects with various market data providers for real-time and historical data.
- **[Order Routing](../o/order_routing.md)**: Direct connectivity to multiple exchanges and brokers for order execution.
- **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md) Tools**: Real-time [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) and monitoring capabilities.
- **[Backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) Module**: Tools for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) using historical market data.

## Integrations
Marketcetera integrates with a wide range of data providers, brokers, and other financial services to enhance its functionality. Some notable integrations include:

- **Market Data Providers**: Integration with leading market data providers for real-time and historical data.
- **Brokerage Firms**: Connectivity with multiple brokers for seamless order execution and management.
- **Exchanges**: Direct integration with various global exchanges for [multi-asset trading](../m/multi-asset_trading.md).
- **APIs and SDKs**: Support for custom integrations and extensions through APIs and SDKs.

## Community and Support
Marketcetera offers extensive support through detailed documentation, user guides, and an active community forum. Users can also access webinars, tutorials, and professional services for implementation and customization assistance.

- **Institutional Traders**: Supports institutional trading desks with a scalable and customizable trading platform.
