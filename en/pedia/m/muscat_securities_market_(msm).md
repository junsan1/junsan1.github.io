# Muscat Securities Market (MSM)

The Muscat Securities [Market](../m/market.md) (MSM), established in 1988, is the [principal](../p/principal.md) stock [exchange](../e/exchange.md) of Oman, located in the [capital](../c/capital.md) city of Muscat. It provides an official platform for trading [stocks](../s/stock.md), bonds, and other securities within the country. Over the years, it has played a significant role in promoting a transparent and organized marketplace, fostering the economic development of Oman by channeling investment to productive sectors.

## Introduction to MSM

As Oman's premier securities [exchange](../e/exchange.md), MSM lists a wide [range](../r/range.md) of companies, predominantly from the regional [economy](../e/economy.md). Its inception served the need to create a structured environment for securities trading, enhancing [market efficiency](../m/market_efficiency.md), [transparency](../t/transparency.md), and [investor](../i/investor.md) protection. The MSM adheres to strict regulations and operates under the supervision of the [Capital](../c/capital.md) [Market](../m/market.md) Authority (CMA) of Oman. 

## Structure and Divisions

### Main Board
The primary [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) in the Muscat Securities [Market](../m/market.md), the Main Board, includes large-cap companies from various sectors such as banking, telecommunications, and [industry](../i/industry.md). Companies [listed](../l/listed.md) here are required to adhere to stringent reporting and operational standards.

### Parallel Market
For smaller and mid-sized companies with potential growth, MSM offers the Parallel [Market](../m/market.md). This provides these enterprises with access to [capital](../c/capital.md) while allowing investors to diversify their portfolios with higher growth prospects.

### Third Market
The Third [Market](../m/market.md) caters to non-[listed](../l/listed.md) public [joint](../j/joint.md)-stock companies and investment funds, [offering](../o/offering.md) an additional layer of investment opportunities. This segment is particularly attractive for seasoned investors looking for niche [market](../m/market.md) opportunities.

## Trading Mechanism

### Order Types
MSM allows various [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md) to cater to different [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md), including:
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Orders:** Executed at the best available price.
- **Limit Orders:** Executed at a specified price or better.
- **Stop Orders:** Activated when the price reaches a specific level.
- **Fill or [Kill](../k/kill.md) Orders:** Either executed immediately in full or canceled.

### Trading Hours
The trading sessions of MSM typically occur between Sunday to Thursday, with [pre-market](../p/pre-market.md) sessions to prepare for the day's trading and post-[market](../m/market.md) sessions to settle transactions. Standard trading hours are from 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM local time.

## Regulatory Framework

The Muscat Securities [Market](../m/market.md) operates under the regulatory oversight of the [Capital](../c/capital.md) [Market](../m/market.md) Authority, which ensures compliance with financial regulations and protects [investor](../i/investor.md) interests. Key regulatory frameworks include:

- **[Disclosure](../d/disclosure.md) Requirements:** [Listed](../l/listed.md) companies must regularly disclose [financial statements](../f/financial_statements.md), significant transactions, and other material information.
- **[Corporate Governance](../c/corporate_governance.md):** Emphasizes ethical practices, accountability, and [transparency](../t/transparency.md) to protect [investor](../i/investor.md) interests.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Conduct:** Ensures trading is conducted in a fair, orderly, and transparent manner.

## Market Indices

### MSM 30 Index
The flagship [index](../i/index.md) of MSM, the MSM 30, tracks the performance of the top 30 companies [listed](../l/listed.md) on the [exchange](../e/exchange.md). This [index](../i/index.md) is a vital [indicator](../i/indicator.md) of [market](../m/market.md) health and [investor](../i/investor.md) sentiment, encompassing diverse sectors.

### Sector Indices
Sector-specific indices give a segmented view of [market](../m/market.md) performance, aiding investors in analyzing sectoral trends and making informed decisions. Prominent [sector indices](../s/sector_indices.md) include:

- **[Financial Sector](../f/financial_sector.md) [Index](../i/index.md)**
- **Industrial Sector [Index](../i/index.md)**
- **Services Sector [Index](../i/index.md)**

## Technological Infrastructure

The MSM employs sophisticated trading platforms to ensure efficient and reliable operations. This includes:

- **Electronic Trading System (ETS):** Facilitates real-time [trade](../t/trade.md) [execution](../e/execution.md) and [order](../o/order.md) matching.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Surveillance System:** Monitors trading activity to detect and prevent [market manipulation](../m/market_manipulation.md) and [insider trading](../i/insider.md).
- **[Clearing and Settlement](../c/clearing_and_settlement.md) System:** Ensures timely and secure [transaction](../t/transaction.md) settlement.
