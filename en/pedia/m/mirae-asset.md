# Mirae Asset

## Overview
Mirae Asset is a global financial services group offering a wide range of investment solutions, including asset management, [wealth management](../w/wealth_management.md), [brokerage services](../b/brokerage_services.md), and investment banking. Known for its robust research capabilities and innovative financial products, Mirae Asset caters to individual investors, institutional clients, and corporations worldwide.

## Features
1. **Wide Range of Instruments**: Access to equities, bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md), and alternative investments.
2. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers multiple trading platforms with advanced charting tools, real-time data, and [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md).
3. **Research and Analysis**: Provides comprehensive research reports, market news, and analysis to support informed investment decisions.
4. **[Wealth Management](../w/wealth_management.md)**: Personalized [wealth management](../w/wealth_management.md) services, including [portfolio management](../p/portfolio_management.md) and investment advisory.
5. **Asset Management**: Expertise in managing mutual funds, ETFs, and institutional assets.
6. **Corporate Finance**: Services in corporate finance, including mergers and acquisitions, equity capital markets, and debt capital markets.
7. **Educational Resources**: Educational materials including webinars, tutorials, articles, and market analysis to help investors improve their skills.
8. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile apps for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
9. **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md) Tools**: Includes [stop-loss orders](../s/stop-loss_orders.md), take-profit orders, and [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) tools.
10. **Global Presence**: Strong presence and expertise in key global markets.
11. **Secure and Regulated**: Mirae Asset is regulated by top-tier financial authorities, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).
