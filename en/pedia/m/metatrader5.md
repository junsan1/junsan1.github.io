# MetaTrader 5 (MT5)

## Overview
MetaTrader 5 (MT5) is a [multi-asset trading](../m/multi-asset_trading.md) platform developed by MetaQuotes Software, [offering](../o/offering.md) advanced trading functionalities and tools for comprehensive [market](../m/market.md) analysis. MT5 is the successor to the popular MetaTrader 4 (MT4) platform and provides enhanced features, making it suitable for trading a wide [range](../r/range.md) of financial instruments, including forex, [stocks](../s/stock.md), [futures](../f/futures.md), and cryptocurrencies.

## Features
1. **[Multi-Asset Trading](../m/multi-asset_trading.md)**: Supports trading across various [asset](../a/asset.md) classes, including forex, [stocks](../s/stock.md), [futures](../f/futures.md), and cryptocurrencies.
2. **Advanced Charting**: Offers a wide [range](../r/range.md) of chart types, 21 timeframes, and over 80 [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md) and analysis tools.
3. **Automated Trading**: Supports automated trading through Expert Advisors (EAs) using the MQL5 programming language.
4. **[Market Depth](../m/market_depth.md)**: Provides detailed [market depth](../m/market_depth.md) information for better understanding of [market](../m/market.md) [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md).
5. **[Economic Calendar](../e/economic_calendar.md)**: Integrated [economic calendar](../e/economic_calendar.md) with real-time updates on important economic events and news.
6. **[Order Types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md)**: Supports [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md), including [market](../m/market.md), limit, stop, and [trailing stop](../t/trailing_stop.md) orders.
7. **Hedging and [Netting](../n/netting.md)**: Offers both hedging and [netting](../n/netting.md) account types to suit different [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
8. **Multi-Device Support**: Available on desktop, web, and mobile devices, ensuring seamless access to trading accounts.
9. **[Fundamental Analysis](../f/fundamental_analysis.md)**: Tools for [fundamental analysis](../f/fundamental_analysis.md), including financial news and [economic indicators](../e/economic_indicators.md).
10. **[Backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) and [Optimization](../o/optimization.md)**: Advanced strategy tester for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) and optimizing [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) using historical data.

## Key Components
- **Trading Terminal**: The main interface for placing orders, viewing [market](../m/market.md) data, and analyzing charts.
- **Navigator**: Access to accounts, indicators, Expert Advisors, and scripts.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Watch**: Real-time quotes for selected trading instruments.
- **Charting Tools**: Advanced charting package with various chart types and [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md) tools.
- **Expert Advisors (EAs)**: [Automated trading systems](../a/automated_trading_systems.md) that execute trades based on pre-defined rules.
- **Strategy Tester**: Tool for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) and optimizing [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) using historical data.
- **MetaEditor**: Integrated development environment for creating custom indicators, scripts, and EAs using MQL5.
- **[Economic Calendar](../e/economic_calendar.md)**: Real-time updates on economic events and news.

## Integrations
MT5 integrates with a variety of brokers, data providers, and financial services to provide a seamless trading experience. Some notable integrations include:

- **Brokerage Firms**: Direct integration with numerous brokers for [order](../o/order.md) [execution](../e/execution.md) and account management.
- **Data Providers**: Access to real-time and historical data from various [market](../m/market.md) data providers.
- **Third-Party Plugins**: Support for various third-party plugins and add-ons to extend the platform’s capabilities.
- **[Trading Signals](../t/trading_signals.md)**: Integration with trading signal services for copy trading.
- **VPS Services**: Integration with Virtual Private Server (VPS) services for 24/7 automated trading.

## Community and Support
MT5 has a large and active community of traders and developers. MetaQuotes provides extensive support through detailed documentation, user guides, and an online community forum. Additionally, users can access a wide [range](../r/range.md) of educational resources, including webinars, tutorials, and a marketplace for trading tools and EAs.

## Use Cases
- **Retail Traders**: Widely used by retail traders for its ease of use and comprehensive trading tools.
- **Day Traders**: Supports day traders with real-time data, advanced charting, and fast [execution](../e/execution.md).
- **Algorithmic Traders**: Enables algorithmic traders to develop, backtest, and deploy automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) using EAs.
- **Multi-[Asset](../a/asset.md) Traders**: Ideal for traders looking to diversify their portfolios across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [asset](../a/asset.md) classes.
- **Brokers**: Utilized by brokers to [offer](../o/offer.md) trading services to their clients with a reliable and versatile platform.

MetaTrader 5’s enhanced features, multi-[asset](../a/asset.md) support, and [robust](../r/robust.md) trading tools make it an ideal choice for traders and brokers seeking a powerful and versatile [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md).
