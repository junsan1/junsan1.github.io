# MarketAxess

## Overview
MarketAxess is a leading electronic [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) for fixed-[income](../i/income.md) securities, providing a marketplace for trading corporate bonds, [municipal bonds](../m/municipal_bonds.md), emerging [market](../m/market.md) [debt](../d/debt.md), and other fixed-[income](../i/income.md) instruments. MarketAxess offers a comprehensive suite of trading solutions, data products, and [market](../m/market.md) insights to institutional investors and dealers, enhancing [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), [transparency](../t/transparency.md), and [efficiency](../e/efficiency.md) in the fixed-[income](../i/income.md) markets.

## Features
1. **Electronic Trading**: Advanced electronic [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) for corporate bonds, [municipal bonds](../m/municipal_bonds.md), and other fixed-[income](../i/income.md) securities.
2. **[Liquidity Pools](../l/liquidity_pools.md)**: Access to deep [liquidity pools](../l/liquidity_pools.md) through an extensive network of global participants, including institutional investors and dealers.
3. **[Open](../o/open.md) Trading**: Innovative all-to-all trading solution allowing [buy-side](../b/buy-side.md) and [sell-side](../s/sell-side.md) firms to [trade](../t/trade.md) directly with each other.
4. **[Market](../m/market.md) Data and Analytics**: Comprehensive [market](../m/market.md) data and analytics tools for pre-[trade](../t/trade.md) and [post-trade analysis](../p/post-trade_analysis.md), including pricing, [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) metrics, and [trade](../t/trade.md) [execution](../e/execution.md) insights.
5. **Auto-[Execution](../e/execution.md)**: Automated [execution](../e/execution.md) tools for streamlining the trading process and improving [efficiency](../e/efficiency.md).
6. **Request for [Quote](../q/quote.md) (RFQ)**: Efficient RFQ process for [price discovery](../p/price_discovery.md) and [trade](../t/trade.md) [execution](../e/execution.md) in illiquid markets.
7. **Compliance and Reporting**: Tools for regulatory compliance and reporting, including [transaction cost analysis](../t/transaction_cost_analysis.md) and best [execution](../e/execution.md) reports.
8. **Portfolio Trading**: Solutions for executing large portfolios of fixed-[income](../i/income.md) securities efficiently.
9. **Integration**: Seamless integration with [order management systems](../o/order_management_systems.md) (OMS) and [execution](../e/execution.md) management systems (EMS) for streamlined workflows.
10. **[Fixed Income](../f/fixed_income.md) Data**: Access to high-quality fixed-[income](../i/income.md) [market](../m/market.md) data, including real-time and historical prices, [spreads](../s/spreads.md), and [yield](../y/yield.md) curves.
