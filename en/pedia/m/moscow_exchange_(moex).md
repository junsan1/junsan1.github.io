# Moscow Exchange (MOEX)

The Moscow [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (MOEX) is one of the largest and most significant financial institutions in Russia, providing a platform for trading in equities, bonds, [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md), currencies, [money](../m/money.md) markets, and commodities. It was established in December 2011 through the [merger](../m/merger.md) of two significant Russian trading platforms: the MICEX (Moscow Interbank [Currency Exchange](../c/currency_exchange.md)) and the RTS (Russian Trading System).

## History and Formation

The Moscow [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) was formed to consolidate the trading of different [asset](../a/asset.md) classes and to enhance the technical and operational aspects of securities trading in Russia. The [merger](../m/merger.md) between MICEX and RTS was a strategic decision aimed at improving [efficiency](../e/efficiency.md), reducing costs, and making the Russian [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md) more attractive to both domestic and international investors.

## Structure and Operations

MOEX operates several markets, including:

### Equities Market

The equities [market](../m/market.md) on MOEX deals with the trading of [shares](../s/shares.md) of public companies. It provides various indices that serve as benchmarks for the Russian [stock market](../s/stock_market.md), including:

- **MOEX Russia [Index](../i/index.md)**: A [capitalization](../c/capitalization.md)-[weighted](../w/weighted.md) [index](../i/index.md) of the most [liquid](../l/liquid.md) Russian [stocks](../s/stock.md).
- **RTS [Index](../i/index.md)**: Measures the performance of the 50 most [liquid](../l/liquid.md) Russian [stocks](../s/stock.md) that [trade](../t/trade.md) on the [exchange](../e/exchange.md).

### Bond Market

MOEX offers trading in both corporate and government bonds. The [bond market](../b/bond_market.md) provides a platform for fundraising by Russian companies and the government. Key segments of the [bond market](../b/bond_market.md) include:

- **Government bonds**: Bonds issued by the Russian Federation, known as OFZ (Federal [Loan](../l/loan.md) [Obligations](../o/obligation.md)).
- **Corporate bonds**: [Debt](../d/debt.md) securities issued by Russian corporations.

### Derivatives Market

MOEX's [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md) [market](../m/market.md) allows trading of [futures](../f/futures.md) and [options](../o/options.md) on various [underlying](../u/underlying.md) assets, including:

- **Stock indices**: [Futures](../f/futures.md) on MOEX Russia [Index](../i/index.md) and RTS [Index](../i/index.md).
- **Individual [stocks](../s/stock.md)**: [Futures](../f/futures.md) and [options](../o/options.md) on [shares](../s/shares.md) of Russian companies.
- **FX rates**: [Futures](../f/futures.md) and [options](../o/options.md) on [currency](../c/currency.md) pairs.
- **Commodities**: [Futures](../f/futures.md) on [crude oil](../c/crude_oil.md), gold, and other commodities.

### Foreign Exchange Market

MOEX offers a platform for trading in various [currency](../c/currency.md) pairs, including the Russian Ruble (RUB) against major global currencies like the US Dollar (USD), [Euro](../e/euro.md) (EUR), Swiss Franc (CHF), and others. This [market](../m/market.md) plays a crucial role in the Russian [economy](../e/economy.md) by providing [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) and facilitating international [trade](../t/trade.md).

### Money Market

The [money market](../m/money_market.md) on MOEX allows participants to [trade](../t/trade.md) [short-term debt](../s/short-term_debt.md) securities and manage their [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md). It includes instruments such as:

- **Repurchase agreements (repos)**: Short-term borrowing agreements where securities are sold with a commitment to repurchase them at a later date.
- **Deposits**: Time deposits offered by financial institutions.

### Commodities Market

The commodities [market](../m/market.md) at MOEX includes trading in precious metals, agricultural products, and energy commodities. Some key commodities traded include:

- **Gold and Silver**: Precious metals that serve as a [store of value](../s/store_of_value.md) and investment.
- **Oil and Gas**: Energy commodities crucial to the Russian [economy](../e/economy.md).
- **Grains**: Agricultural products like wheat and barley.

## Technology and Infrastructure

MOEX has invested significantly in technology to ensure seamless and efficient trading. The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) operates advanced trading platforms that provide low latency and high [throughput](../t/throughput.md), essential for modern [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md). Some of the key technological features include:

- **ASTS (Automated System of Trading and Settlement)**: The primary [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) used for equities, bonds, and [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md).
- **Equities and [Bond Market](../b/bond_market.md) Engine (EQM)**: Specialized trading engine for equities and bonds.
- **FORTS**: [Futures](../f/futures.md) and [Options](../o/options.md) Russian Trading System, the [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md) [market](../m/market.md) [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md).

## Regulation and Compliance

MOEX operates under the regulatory oversight of the Central [Bank](../b/bank.md) of Russia, ensuring that trading activities comply with national financial laws and regulations. The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) has also implemented measures to adhere to international standards and [best practices](../b/best_practices.md) to enhance [market](../m/market.md) integrity and [investor](../i/investor.md) confidence.

## Market Participants

The Moscow [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) caters to a wide [range](../r/range.md) of [market](../m/market.md) participants, including:

- **Domestic and international investors**: Both retail and institutional investors participate in trading activities.
- **Brokerage firms**: Intermediaries that facilitate trading for investors.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) makers**: Participants that provide [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) by quoting buy and sell prices.
- **[Clearing and settlement](../c/clearing_and_settlement.md) institutions**: Organizations that ensure the smooth completion of trades.

## Strategic Initiatives

MOEX has undertaken several strategic initiatives to strengthen its position as a leading financial marketplace:

- **[Market](../m/market.md) development**: Expanding product offerings and improving [market](../m/market.md) accessibility.
- **Technological advancements**: Continuously upgrading trading platforms and [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md).
- **International partnerships**: Collaborating with global exchanges and financial institutions to attract [foreign investment](../f/foreign_investment.md).
- **Financial education**: Promoting [financial literacy](../f/financial_literacy.md) among retail investors through educational programs and outreach activities.

## Access and Admission

To [trade](../t/trade.md) on the Moscow [Exchange](../e/exchange.md), participants need to meet specific requirements and obtain necessary permissions. This process involves:

- **Registration**: Participants must register with MOEX and obtain a trading code.
- **Brokerage accounts**: Investors need to [open](../o/open.md) accounts with brokerage firms that are members of the [exchange](../e/exchange.md).
- **Compliance**: Adhering to regulatory requirements and internal compliance procedures.
