# Hargreaves Lansdown

## Overview
Hargreaves Lansdown is a leading provider of investment services and financial products in the UK. The company offers a wide [range](../r/range.md) of services including stockbroking, [investment management](../i/investment_management.md), [retirement planning](../r/retirement_planning.md), and financial advice. Known for its comprehensive platform, Hargreaves Lansdown caters to individual investors, financial advisors, and corporate clients.

## Features
1. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Investment [Options](../o/options.md)**: Access to [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, mutual funds, bonds, [options](../o/options.md), and more.
2. **Trading Platforms**: Offers a [robust](../r/robust.md) online [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) with advanced charting tools, research, and analysis.
3. **Investment Accounts**: Provides various account types including ISAs, SIPPs, and general investment accounts.
4. **[Retirement Planning](../r/retirement_planning.md)**: Comprehensive [retirement planning](../r/retirement_planning.md) services including SIPPs and annuities.
5. **[Wealth Management](../w/wealth_management.md)**: Personalized [wealth management](../w/wealth_management.md) and advisory services.
6. **Research and Insights**: Access to in-depth research reports, [market](../m/market.md) analysis, and expert commentary.
7. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials including webinars, articles, and investment guides.
8. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile app for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading and account management on the go.
9. **Competitive Pricing**: Low-cost trading commissions and fees with transparent pricing.
10. **[Customer](../c/customer.md) Support**: High-quality [customer](../c/customer.md) support available via phone, email, and live chat.
