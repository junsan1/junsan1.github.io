# XT.COM

[XT](../x/xt.md).COM is a digital cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) platform designed to provide users with a safe, efficient, and transparent [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md). Launched in 2018, the [exchange](../e/exchange.md) has established itself as a [robust](../r/robust.md) platform with a broad [range](../r/range.md) of services catering to various types of investors and traders. [XT](../x/xt.md).COM combines a user-friendly interface, advanced [security](../s/security.md) features, and an extensive selection of digital assets, making it a prominent player in the cryptocurrency trading ecosystem.

## Overview

[XT](../x/xt.md).COM offers a comprehensive suite of services for its users, including spot trading, [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md) trading, [margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md), and various financial services like staking and savings. The platform supports a vast number of cryptocurrencies, enabling users to [trade](../t/trade.md) and invest across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) token pairs. The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) is particularly known for its focus on both popular cryptocurrencies like [Bitcoin](../b/bitcoin.md) (BTC) and [Ethereum](../e/ethereum_.md) (ETH), as well as a variety of altcoins and emerging tokens.

### Key Features

1. **User-Friendly Interface:** [XT](../x/xt.md).COM provides an intuitive interface that allows both novice and experienced traders to navigate and execute trades easily.
2. **[Security](../s/security.md):** The platform employs advanced [security](../s/security.md) measures, including two-[factor](../f/factor.md) authentication (2FA), cold storage for the majority of user funds, and regular [security](../s/security.md) audits.
3. **[Liquidity](../l/liquidity.md):** By maintaining high [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) for various trading pairs, [XT](../x/xt.md).COM ensures that trades can be executed swiftly and at competitive prices.
4. **Mobile App:** Comprehensive mobile applications for both Android and iOS, allowing users to [trade](../t/trade.md) on the go.
5. **[Customer](../c/customer.md) Support:** 24/7 [customer](../c/customer.md) support is available through various channels, including live chat and email, to assist users with any issues or inquiries.

## Trading Options

[XT](../x/xt.md).COM offers various trading [options](../o/options.md) to cater to different [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) and [risk](../r/risk.md) appetites:

### Spot Trading

Spot trading on [XT](../x/xt.md).COM involves the buying and selling of cryptocurrencies at current [market](../m/market.md) prices. Users can place [market](../m/market.md) orders, limit orders, and stop orders to manage their trades. The platform provides detailed charts and analytical tools to help traders make informed decisions.

### Margin Trading

[Margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md) allows users to borrow funds to [trade](../t/trade.md) larger positions than they currently [hold](../h/hold.md). This can amplify gains but also increases the [risk](../r/risk.md) of significant losses. [XT](../x/xt.md).COM offers variable [leverage](../l/leverage.md) [options](../o/options.md) depending on the cryptocurrency pair being traded. Proper [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) and understanding of [margin](../m/margin.md) requirements are crucial for [margin](../m/margin.md) traders.

Additionally, [XT](../x/xt.md).COM offers savings products, enabling users to earn [interest](../i/interest.md) on their idle assets.

## Security Measures

[XT](../x/xt.md).COM prioritizes the [security](../s/security.md) of users' funds and personal information. To ensure a high level of [security](../s/security.md), the platform has implemented [multiple](../m/multiple.md) measures:

1. **Cold Storage:** The majority of user funds are stored in cold wallets, which are offline and significantly less vulnerable to hacking.
2. **Two-[Factor](../f/factor.md) Authentication (2FA):** Enables an additional layer of [security](../s/security.md) for user accounts.
3. **SSL Encryption:** All data transmitted on the platform is secured with SSL encryption to protect against interception.
4. **Regular [Security](../s/security.md) Audits:** The platform undergoes frequent [security](../s/security.md) assessments to identify and mitigate potential vulnerabilities.
