# Luno

Luno is a leading global cryptocurrency platform that provides a simple and secure way to buy, store, and trade various cryptocurrencies. Founded in 2013 by Marcus Swanepoel and Timothy Stranex, Luno has grown to serve millions of users across more than 40 countries. The platform's primary focus is on enabling users to invest in cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Ripple, offering a suite of features designed to streamline the trading process for both beginners and advanced traders.

## Company Overview

The company's mission is to upgrade the world to a better financial system. Luno operates with the belief that cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology can revolutionize the global financial system by making it more inclusive, efficient, and accessible. Their user-friendly platform provides a way for individuals to enter the cryptocurrency space smoothly and securely.

Luno's headquarters are in London, UK, but it has offices in several other major cities, including Singapore and Cape Town. The company is regulated and complies with the local laws and regulations in the regions it operates.

**Website**: [Luno](https://www.luno.com)

## Key Features

### User-Friendly Interface
Luno's platform is designed with simplicity in mind, making it accessible even for people who are new to cryptocurrencies. The intuitive user interface allows users to navigate the platform easily, from setting up an account to executing trades.

### Mobile and Desktop Apps
Luno offers both mobile and desktop applications, providing users with flexibility and convenience to manage their cryptocurrency investments anywhere, anytime. The mobile app is available for both Android and iOS devices, while the desktop version can be accessed through any modern web browser.

### Secure Wallets
Security is a top priority for Luno. The platform provides secure wallets for storing cryptocurrencies, which include advanced security features like multi-signature wallets, cold storage, and two-factor authentication (2FA).

### Instant Buy/Sell
Luno's Instant Buy/Sell feature allows users to easily purchase or sell cryptocurrencies at the current market rate. This is an ideal service for beginners who may not be familiar with the intricacies of cryptocurrency trading.

### Trading Platform
For advanced traders, Luno offers a comprehensive trading platform that includes features such as charting tools, order book, and custom order types. This platform is designed to meet the needs of experienced traders who require more sophisticated trading capabilities.

### Luno Learning Portal
Luno provides an extensive learning portal that includes articles, guides, and video tutorials aimed at educating users about cryptocurrencies and blockchain technology. The goal is to equip users with the knowledge they need to make informed investment decisions.

### Luno Business
For businesses, Luno offers a dedicated service that enables them to accept, store, and spend cryptocurrencies. This is ideal for companies looking to integrate cryptocurrencies into their payment systems or treasury functions.

## Security Measures

### Multi-Signature Technology
Luno employs multi-signature technology to ensure the safety of users' assets. This adds an extra layer of security by requiring multiple signatures for a transaction to be executed.

### Cold Storage
A significant portion of users' funds is kept in cold storage, which means they are not connected to the internet and are therefore protected from online threats and hacking attempts.

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
Luno strongly recommends that users enable two-factor authentication (2FA) to add an additional layer of security to their accounts. This involves using a secondary device or app to verify the user's identity during the login process.

### Regular Audits
Luno conducts regular security audits to identify and mitigate any potential vulnerabilities. The platform also complies with industry best practices for cyber security.

### Regulatory Compliance
Luno adheres to the local laws and regulations in the jurisdictions it operates. This includes registration with applicable financial authorities and compliance with anti-money laundering (AML) and know your customer (KYC) requirements.

## Buying and Selling Cryptocurrencies

### Account Setup
To start using Luno, users need to set up an account by providing their basic information and completing the KYC verification process. This typically involves submitting a government-issued ID and proof of residence.

### Deposits and Withdrawals
Users can fund their Luno accounts through various methods, including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and supported cryptocurrencies. Withdrawals can also be made in local fiat currencies or cryptocurrencies.

### Trading Process
Luno simplifies the trading process with its Instant Buy/Sell feature, which allows users to quickly purchase or sell cryptocurrencies at the current market price. For advanced traders, Luno's trading platform provides additional functionalities such as limit and stop orders.

### Fees and Limits
Luno charges a fee for transactions, which varies based on the type of trade and the payment method used. The platform's fee structure is transparent, with detailed information provided on their website. Additionally, there are daily limits on transactions, which can be increased by completing higher levels of verification.
