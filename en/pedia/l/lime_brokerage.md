# Lime Brokerage

## Overview
Lime Brokerage is a leading provider of advanced trading technologies and [execution](../e/execution.md) services, specializing in low-latency trading solutions. The company offers direct [market](../m/market.md) access (DMA) to a wide [range](../r/range.md) of financial instruments, including equities, [options](../o/options.md), and [futures](../f/futures.md). Known for its high-speed trading [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md), Lime Brokerage caters to professional traders, [hedge](../h/hedge.md) funds, and [proprietary trading](../p/proprietary_trading.md) firms.

## Features
1. **Low-Latency Trading**: Advanced trading [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md) designed for low-latency [execution](../e/execution.md), ensuring fast and efficient [trade](../t/trade.md) processing.
2. **Direct [Market](../m/market.md) Access (DMA)**: Provides direct access to major U.S. exchanges and [dark pools](../d/dark_pools.md), enabling high-speed trading.
3. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to equities, [options](../o/options.md), and [futures](../f/futures.md) markets.
4. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers [multiple](../m/multiple.md) trading platforms with advanced charting tools, real-time data, and [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md).
5. **[Algorithmic Trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md)**: Supports automated and [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) strategies through [robust](../r/robust.md) APIs.
6. **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md) Tools**: Comprehensive [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) tools to monitor and manage trading risks effectively.
7. **Customizable Solutions**: Tailored trading solutions to meet the specific needs of institutional clients.
8. **Secure and Regulated**: Lime Brokerage is regulated by top-tier financial authorities, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).
9. **Research and Analysis**: Access to in-depth [market](../m/market.md) data, research reports, and analysis tools.
10. **[Customer](../c/customer.md) Support**: Dedicated [customer](../c/customer.md) support available to assist clients with their trading and technical needs.
