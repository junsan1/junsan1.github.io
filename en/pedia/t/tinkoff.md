# Tinkoff

## Overview
Tinkoff is a leading online financial ecosystem in Russia, [offering](../o/offering.md) a wide [range](../r/range.md) of financial services including banking, brokerage, [insurance](../i/insurance.md), and lifestyle services. Tinkoff Investments, the brokerage arm of Tinkoff, provides access to a comprehensive suite of investment products and trading platforms, catering to both individual investors and active traders.

## Features
1. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to [stocks](../s/stock.md), bonds, ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), mutual funds, and currencies.
2. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers [multiple](../m/multiple.md) trading platforms including the Tinkoff Investments app and web platform with advanced charting tools and real-time data.
3. **[Commission](../c/commission.md)-Free Trading**: Offers [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading for certain accounts and instruments.
4. **[Market Research](../m/market_research.md) and Analysis**: Access to comprehensive research reports, [market](../m/market.md) news, and analysis tools.
5. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including webinars, tutorials, articles, and [market](../m/market.md) analysis to help traders improve their skills.
6. **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md)**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
7. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile app for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
8. **Integrated Ecosystem**: Seamless integration with Tinkoff's banking and financial services.
9. **[Customer](../c/customer.md) Support**: Dedicated [customer](../c/customer.md) support available to assist clients with their trading and investment needs.
10. **Secure and Regulated**: Tinkoff is regulated by top-tier financial authorities in Russia, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).

- **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md) Tools**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
