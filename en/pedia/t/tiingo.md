# Tiingo

## Overview
Tiingo is a comprehensive financial data platform that provides high-quality, affordable data services to individual investors, traders, and developers. Known for its extensive data coverage and user-friendly APIs, Tiingo offers real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data, news, and advanced analytics tools, catering to various financial needs, from [portfolio management](../p/portfolio_management.md) to [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md).

## Features
1. **[Real-Time Market Data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Provides fast and accurate real-time data for equities, ETFs, and mutual funds.
2. **Historical Data**: Extensive historical data for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md), research, and analysis, including daily, intraday, and [tick](../t/tick.md)-level data.
3. **News and [Sentiment Analysis](../s/sentiment_analysis.md)**: Access to real-time news feeds and [sentiment analysis](../s/sentiment_analysis.md) to stay informed about [market](../m/market.md)-moving events.
4. **Fundamental Data**: Detailed [financial statements](../f/financial_statements.md), ratios, and company profiles for comprehensive analysis.
5. **Advanced Analytics**: Tools for [portfolio management](../p/portfolio_management.md), performance measurement, and [risk analysis](../r/risk_analysis.md).
6. **API Access**: Developer-friendly APIs for seamless data retrieval and integration with financial models and applications.
7. **Data Feeds**: Continuous data feeds for real-time updates and automated processes.
8. **Custom Alerts**: Customizable alerts for price movements, news events, and other [market](../m/market.md) conditions.
9. **Data Quality and Reliability**: High-quality data sourced directly from exchanges and other trusted providers.
10. **Cost-Effective**: Competitive pricing plans to provide affordable access to high-quality financial data.

## Integrations
Tiingo integrates with a variety of financial platforms, data providers, and third-party applications to enhance its functionality. Some notable integrations include:

- **Trading Platforms**: Compatibility with popular trading platforms for seamless data access and [execution](../e/execution.md) capabilities.
- **[Financial Analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md) Tools**: Integration with tools like Microsoft Excel, MATLAB, and R for advanced analysis and visualization.
- **APIs and SDKs**: Provides APIs and SDKs for custom integrations and automated data retrieval.
- **Third-Party Applications**: Support for various third-party financial applications and services.
