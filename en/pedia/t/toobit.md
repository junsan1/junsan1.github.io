## Security Protocols

Toobit has implemented a multi-layered security framework to safeguard user assets and data. The primary components of this framework include:

### 1. Cold Storage
The majority of user funds are stored in cold wallets, significantly reducing the risk of cyber-attacks. Only a small portion of funds are kept in hot wallets to facilitate active trading.

### 2. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
2FA is mandatory for all users, adding an extra layer of security by requiring a second form of verification during the login process.

### 3. Regular Audits
Periodic security audits are conducted to identify and address vulnerabilities within the platform. These audits are performed by reputable third-party firms specializing in cybersecurity.

### 4. Encryption
All sensitive user information is encrypted using advanced cryptographic techniques. This ensures that even in the event of a data breach, the information remains unintelligible to unauthorized parties.

### 5. Anti-Phishing
Toobit employs measures to prevent phishing attempts, including personalized anti-phishing codes and continuous monitoring for suspicious activity.
