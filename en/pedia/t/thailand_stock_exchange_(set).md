# Thailand Stock Exchange (SET)

The Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) of Thailand (SET) is the national stock [exchange](../e/exchange.md) of Thailand, providing a platform for the trading of various types of securities, such as equities, bonds, and [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md). Since its inception, the SET has grown to become a significant player in the Southeast Asian financial [market](../m/market.md). Below is a comprehensive overview of the SET, covering its history, structure, [trading systems](../t/trading_systems.md), key indices, [market](../m/market.md) participants, regulatory framework, and its role in the Thai and global [economy](../e/economy.md).

## History and Overview

The Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) of Thailand (SET) was established on April 30, 1975, following several years of planning and the establishment of the National Economic and Social Development Board (NESDB). The foundation of the SET was an essential step in formalizing Thailand's [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md), which previously existed in a more fragmented and informal manner.

The SET's mission is to foster a [liquid](../l/liquid.md) and efficient [market](../m/market.md) as a fundamental pillar for the country's economic development. It seeks to promote growth in [capital markets](../c/capital_markets.md) through technological advancements, improving [market](../m/market.md) quality, and expanding [investor](../i/investor.md) base, among others.

## Market Structure

### Listed Companies and Sectors

SET houses a diverse [range](../r/range.md) of companies across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) sectors such as Financials, Industrials, Services, Consumer Products, Property, and Technology. This diversity enhances the depth and breadth of the [market](../m/market.md), attracting a wide array of local and international investors.

As of the latest financial year, SET [listed](../l/listed.md) over 600 companies with a combined [market capitalization](../m/market_capitalization.md) surpassing $600 billion. The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) is an ideal platform for [capital](../c/capital.md) raising efforts for both emerging startups and established corporations.

### Trading Mechanism

SET functions as an electronic [order](../o/order.md)-driven [market](../m/market.md), wherein orders are matched by the [exchange](../e/exchange.md)'s trading system called "SET CONNECT." This platform integrates advanced trading technology, facilitating high-speed, efficient transactions. The trading day is divided into several sessions: a pre-opening session, two continuous trading sessions interrupted by a lunch break, and a closing session.

Key trading [statistics](../s/statistics.md) like price, [volume](../v/volume.md), and [value](../v/value.md) of transactions are publicly disclosed, ensuring [transparency](../t/transparency.md) and facilitating informed decision-making.

### Key Indices

Several indices provide comprehensive insights into the performance of the overarching [market](../m/market.md), including but not limited to:

1. **SET [Index](../i/index.md)**: The flagship [index](../i/index.md) that measures the price movements of common [stocks](../s/stock.md) [listed](../l/listed.md) on SET.
2. **SET50**: Represents the 50 largest companies by [market](../m/market.md) cap.
3. **SET100**: Similar to SET50 but includes the top 100 companies.
4. **sSET [Index](../i/index.md)**: Inclusion of smaller [listed](../l/listed.md) companies, complementing the larger indices.
5. **SETCLMV**: Stands for Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, and Vietnam, focusing on companies with significant [business](../b/business.md) operations in these countries.

## Trading Systems and Technology

### SET CONNECT

SET CONNECT, introduced in 2012, is the primary trading system employed by the Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) of Thailand. It integrates pre-[trade](../t/trade.md) [risk management](../r/risk_management.md), [market](../m/market.md) surveillance, and [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md) and interfaces, supporting an [order](../o/order.md)-driven [market](../m/market.md). Leveraging high-speed [order matching algorithms](../o/order_matching_algorithms.md), SET CONNECT ensures minimal latency and high [throughput](../t/throughput.md), accommodating the trading needs of both retail and institutional investors.

SET CONNECT also supports various trading functionalities, including block trading, trading at the turn of [market](../m/market.md) sessions, and various auction mechanisms. This flexibility fosters a resilient [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md) capable of handling diverse [market](../m/market.md) conditions.

### Algorithmic Trading

In recent years, [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) has gained traction on the SET. Utilizing predefined criteria and advanced statistical models, [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) can execute orders at speeds unmatched by human traders. [Market](../m/market.md) participants often use [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) for purposes such as [arbitrage](../a/arbitrage.md), [market](../m/market.md) making, and [trend following strategies](../t/trend_following_strategies.md).

[Algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) systems interface seamlessly with SET CONNECT, leveraging its high-speed capabilities to [capitalize](../c/capitalize.md) on fleeting [market](../m/market.md) opportunities. Regulatory measures ensure that these high-frequency trading activities do not compromise [market](../m/market.md) fairness and integrity.

## Regulatory Framework

The SET operates under the regulatory oversight of the Securities and [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) [Commission](../c/commission.md) (SEC) of Thailand, established by the Securities and [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) Act of 1992. This legislative framework mandates stringent controls to ensure [market](../m/market.md) [transparency](../t/transparency.md), [investor](../i/investor.md) protection, and efficient functioning of the [capital](../c/capital.md) [market](../m/market.md).

### Key Regulations

1. **[Listing Requirements](../l/listing_requirements.md)**: Detailed criteria govern the eligibility of companies to list on the SET. These include minimum [capital](../c/capital.md) requirements, profitability metrics, and standards of [corporate governance](../c/corporate_governance.md).
2. **[Disclosure](../d/disclosure.md) Standards**: Companies must abide by rigorous [disclosure](../d/disclosure.md) norms, regularly updating the [market](../m/market.md) on [financial performance](../f/financial_performance.md), material developments, and operational milestones.
3. **[Market](../m/market.md) Surveillance**: The SEC employs advanced surveillance mechanisms to detect and prevent [market](../m/market.md) manipulations, [insider trading](../i/insider.md), and other malpractices.

### Ethical and Sustainable Investing

In alignment with global [best practices](../b/best_practices.md), SET emphasizes the importance of Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria. Companies are encouraged to adopt sustainable [business](../b/business.md) practices, contributing to broader societal goals.

## Key Participants and Institutional Structure

### Retail Investors

Retail investors form a significant portion of the trading [volume](../v/volume.md) on the SET. Education campaigns and simplified trading platforms have been implemented to foster greater retail participation and [financial literacy](../f/financial_literacy.md).

### Institutional Investors

Institutional investors, including mutual funds, pension funds, and foreign investors, play a critical role in the SET. These participants bring in substantial [capital](../c/capital.md) and [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), enhancing the overall stability and depth of the [market](../m/market.md).

### Brokers and Market Makers

Licensed brokers act as intermediaries, facilitating transactions between buyers and sellers. [Market](../m/market.md) makers provide [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) by continually quoting buy and sell prices for designated securities, playing a pivotal role in maintaining [market](../m/market.md) fluidity.

## Economic Impact and Global Positioning

### Domestic Role

The SET is a cornerstone of Thailand's [economy](../e/economy.md), providing a platform for [capital](../c/capital.md) accumulation, resource allocation, and [wealth](../w/wealth.md) generation. Companies raise [capital](../c/capital.md) for [expansion](../e/expansion.md) projects, job creation, and innovation initiatives, driving overall [economic growth](../e/economic_growth.md).

### International Integration

Increasingly, the SET has positioned itself as a competitive player in global E-[financing](../f/financing.md). This includes partnerships with other exchanges, cross-listings, and avenues facilitating [foreign investment](../f/foreign_investment.md) inflows. The [market](../m/market.md) openness enhances Thailand's [visibility](../v/visibility.md) and attractiveness as an investment destination.

### Sustainable Development and Innovation

The SET takes proactive measures to foster innovation and sustainability practices, aligning with international benchmarks like the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). [Green bonds](../g/green_bonds.md), social impact funds, and sustainability-linked products are among the instruments offered.
