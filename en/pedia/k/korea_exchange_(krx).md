# Korea Exchange (KRX)

The Korea [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (KRX) is the sole securities [exchange](../e/exchange.md) operator in South Korea, providing a comprehensive [range](../r/range.md) of products and services to domestic and international investors. The KRX was established in 2005 through the [merger](../m/merger.md) of the Korea Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md), Korea [Futures](../f/futures.md) [Exchange](../e/exchange.md), and KOSDAQ [Stock Market](../s/stock_market.md). This historic [merger](../m/merger.md) aimed to enhance [market efficiency](../m/market_efficiency.md) and [investor](../i/investor.md) accessibility while fostering global competitiveness.

KRX operates under the oversight of the Financial Services [Commission](../c/commission.md) (FSC) of South Korea, playing a crucial role in the financial [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md) of the country. Its main office is located in Busan, South Korea's second-largest city, with additional offices in Seoul.

Key Components of Korea [Exchange](../e/exchange.md):
1. **KOSPI [Market](../m/market.md)**: This is the main board of the Korea [Exchange](../e/exchange.md), which includes major [listed](../l/listed.md) companies in South Korea. KOSPI stands for Korean Composite Stock Price [Index](../i/index_instrument.md), representing the overall performance of the [exchange](../e/exchange.md). The KOSPI [index](../i/index_instrument.md) is one of the key indicators of the South Korean [economy](../e/economy.md).

2. **KOSDAQ [Market](../m/market.md)**: Short for Korean Securities Dealers Automated Quotations, this [market](../m/market.md) is designed for small to medium-sized businesses and startups. It is often compared to the [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md) in the United States and provides a platform for young, high-growth potential companies to raise [capital](../c/capital.md).

3. **KONEX [Market](../m/market.md)**: The Korea New [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (KONEX) caters to micro and small-cap companies, aiming to provide them with opportunities to scale and eventually progress to KOSDAQ or KOSPI. This was introduced to facilitate [venture capital](../v/venture_capital.md) and angel [investing](../i/investing.md) specifically in early-stage ventures.

4. **[Derivatives](../d/derivatives.md) [Market](../m/market.md)**: The KRX operates a [robust](../r/robust.md) [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md) [market](../m/market.md), which includes a wide array of [futures](../f/futures.md) and [options](../o/options.md). Products traded include single stock [futures](../f/futures.md), [index futures](../i/index_futures.md) (such as the KOSPI 200 [futures](../f/futures.md)), and [interest rate futures](../i/interest_rate_futures.md), among others.

5. **[Bond Market](../b/bond_market.md)**: The KRX also provides a platform for trading government and corporate bonds. This aspect of the KRX supports the fixed-[income](../i/income.md) segment of the [market](../m/market.md), [offering](../o/offering.md) various [bond](../b/bond.md) types including treasury bonds, [municipal bonds](../m/municipal_bonds.md), and special-purpose bonds.

## Trading Hours and Sessions

KRX operates on a standard trading schedule from 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM Korea Standard Time (KST) for the [stock market](../s/stock_market.md). However, trading hours can vary for different segments:
- **[Pre-market](../p/pre-market.md) session**: 8:00 AM to 9:00 AM KST
- **Regular [market](../m/market.md) session**: 9:00 AM to 3:30 PM KST
- **Post-[market](../m/market.md) session**: 3:40 PM to 6:00 PM KST

### Information and Trading Systems

1. **EXTURE+**: KRX employs a high-tech trading system known as EXTURE+, which ensures high [efficiency](../e/efficiency.md) and low latency in transactions. This system supports various trading methodologies such as continuous auction, call auction, and negotiated trading.

2. **[Market](../m/market.md) Data**: KRX offers [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md) services including price, [volume](../v/volume.md), and [order book](../o/order_book.md) information. This data is crucial for investors, traders, and [algorithmic trading platforms](../a/algorithmic_trading_platforms.md) to make informed decisions.

### Indexes

1. **KOSPI [Index](../i/index_instrument.md)**: The KOSPI [index](../i/index_instrument.md) is a [capitalization](../c/capitalization.md)-[weighted](../w/weighted.md) [index](../i/index_instrument.md) of all common [stocks](../s/stock.md) [listed](../l/listed.md) on the Korea [Exchange](../e/exchange.md). It serves as the [benchmark](../b/benchmark.md) [index](../i/index_instrument.md) for the South Korean [stock market](../s/stock_market.md).

2. **KOSDAQ [Index](../i/index_instrument.md)**: This [index](../i/index_instrument.md) specifically tracks the performance of all companies [listed](../l/listed.md) on the KOSDAQ [market](../m/market.md).

3. **KOSPI 200**: This is a [derivative](../d/derivative.md) [index](../i/index_instrument.md) based on the top 200 companies in the KOSPI [market](../m/market.md), representing the overall movement and [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) of major [stocks](../s/stock.md).

4. **KRX 100**: An [index](../i/index_instrument.md) that measures the performance of the top 100 blue-chip companies [listed](../l/listed.md) on both KOSPI and KOSDAQ.

## Regulations and Compliance

The KRX operates under stringent regulatory frameworks to ensure transparent, fair, and efficient [market](../m/market.md) operations. The Financial Investment Services and [Capital Markets](../c/capital_markets.md) Act (FSCMA) governs activities on the [exchange](../e/exchange.md). Additionally, KRX adheres to the guidelines set by the International Organization of Securities Commissions (IOSCO) to maintain global [best practices](../b/best_practices.md).

### Listing Requirements

1. **For KOSPI**: Companies must meet specific financial criteria, including minimum [profit](../p/profit.md) and [equity](../e/equity.md) requirements.
2. **For KOSDAQ**: Emphasis is placed on growth potential, making it accessible to startups and smaller businesses.
3. **For KONEX**: Regulations are more lenient compared to KOSPI and KOSDAQ, fostering the ecosystem for early-stage companies.

### Market Surveillance

KRX engages in rigorous [market](../m/market.md) surveillance activities to detect and prevent [market manipulation](../m/market_manipulation.md), [insider trading](../i/insider.md), and other malpractices. Automated systems and algorithms are employed for real-time monitoring of trades and [market](../m/market.md) activities.

## International Collaboration and Cross-Listings

KRX actively collaborates with other international exchanges and has several Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs) to promote cross-border listings and [market](../m/market.md) integration. Some of the exchanges KRX collaborates with include the New York Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (NYSE), Japan [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) Group (JPX), and China’s Shanghai Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md).

## Investment Opportunities

KRX offers various investment vehicles, catering to different [investor](../i/investor.md) profiles:
- **Equities**: Investment in common [stocks](../s/stock.md) of [listed](../l/listed.md) companies.
- **[Exchange](../e/exchange.md)-Traded Funds (ETFs)**: KRX has a broad [range](../r/range.md) of ETFs, providing diversified investment [options](../o/options.md).
- **[Derivatives](../d/derivatives.md)**: [Futures](../f/futures.md) and [options](../o/options.md) for hedging or speculative purposes.
- **[Fixed Income Securities](../f/fixed_income_securities.md)**: Government and corporate bonds for conservative investors.

## Tech Innovations and Algorithmic Trading

[Algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) has become increasingly prevalent on the KRX, with high-frequency trading (HFT) gaining traction due to the deployment of state-of-the-art [trading systems](../t/trading_systems.md) like EXTURE+. Firms engage in various algorithmic strategies including [arbitrage](../a/arbitrage.md), [pairs trading](../p/pairs_trading.md), and [trend following](../t/trend_following.md). The [exchange](../e/exchange.md) provides ample [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) and low-latency [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md) to support such activities.

### Statistical Data
As of the recent period, the KOSPI [index](../i/index_instrument.md) encompasses over 800 companies with a collective [market capitalization](../m/market_capitalization.md) exceeding $1.5 trillion USD. The KOSDAQ [market](../m/market.md) supports over 1,200 companies, emphasizing its role in the growth of South Korea’s tech and biotech sectors.

## Sustainability and ESG

KRX is committed to promoting Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles in corporate practices and [investor](../i/investor.md) selections. It has introduced various ESG reporting guidelines and indexes, such as the KRX ESG Leaders [index](../i/index_instrument.md), to facilitate sustainable investment.

## Conclusion

The Korea [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (KRX) stands as a pivotal institution in South Korea's financial ecosystem. With its multi-faceted markets, stringent regulations, and technological advancements, KRX provides a [robust](../r/robust.md) platform for both domestic and international investors. As South Korea continues to emerge as a global economic power, the importance of KRX in facilitating [capital](../c/capital.md) flows and [economic growth](../e/economic_growth.md) cannot be overstated.
