# Korbit

Korbit is a prominent cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) based in South Korea. Established in 2013, it is renowned for being one of the earliest [Bitcoin](../b/bitcoin.md) exchanges in the country. Korbit plays a pivotal role in the South Korean digital [asset](../a/asset.md) ecosystem by facilitating the [trade](../t/trade.md) of a variety of cryptocurrencies, including [Bitcoin](../b/bitcoin.md), [Ethereum](../e/ethereum_.md), [Ripple](../r/ripple.md), and many others.

Korbit's foundational mission is to revolutionize the way financial transactions are conducted by leveraging [blockchain](../b/blockchain_in_trading.md) technology. Its platform is designed to bridge the gap between traditional financial systems and the burgeoning world of cryptocurrencies, making digital assets more accessible to the average consumer and institutional investors alike. 

## Company Background

Korbit was co-founded by Tony Lyu and Kangmo Kim, along with three other partners. The company received backing from several high-profile investors, including SoftBank Ventures Korea and Pantera [Capital](../c/capital.md), which helped it [gain](../g/gain.md) traction in its early days. Korbit is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea, a region known for its rapid technological advancements and adoption of digital currencies.

Over the years, Korbit has developed a [robust](../r/robust.md) suite of services, including spot trading, wallet services, and comprehensive [customer](../c/customer.md) support. These services cater to both individual traders and institutional clients, ensuring a wide-reaching impact within the cryptocurrency [market](../m/market.md).

## Services and Features

### Spot Trading

One of Korbit's primary services is its spot [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md). This service allows users to buy and sell a wide array of cryptocurrencies in real-time. Spot trading on Korbit is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly, featuring advanced trading tools for more experienced users. It also provides:

- **[Real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Users have access to live price charts, [order](../o/order.md) books, and [trade](../t/trade.md) histories.
- **Limit and [market](../m/market.md) orders**: These [options](../o/options.md) allow users to execute trades at their desired price points.
- **High [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md)**: By maintaining a deep [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) pool, Korbit ensures that trades can be executed quickly and efficiently.

### Wallet Services

Korbit provides secure and reliable cryptocurrency wallet services. The platform's wallet system is designed to safeguard users' digital assets through a combination of hot and cold storage mechanisms. Key features include:

- **Multi-signature wallets**: Enhance [security](../s/security.md) by requiring [multiple](../m/multiple.md) approvals for transactions.
- **Cold storage**: Ensures that the majority of funds are stored offline, reducing the [risk](../r/risk.md) of hacks.
- **[Insurance](../i/insurance.md)**: Some assets are insured against certain types of cyber threats, adding an extra layer of [security](../s/security.md).

### Educational Resources

Understanding that the world of cryptocurrencies can be daunting to newcomers, Korbit offers a variety of educational resources aimed at increasing [customer](../c/customer.md) literacy. These include:

- **Tutorials and guides**: Step-by-step instructions on how to use the platform and [trade](../t/trade.md) cryptocurrencies.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) analysis**: Regular reports and analyses to keep users informed about [market](../m/market.md) trends and developments.
- **Webinars and workshops**: Interactive sessions led by [industry](../i/industry.md) experts.

## Regulatory Compliance

Korbit operates in a heavily regulated environment, particularly with South Korea's stringent financial regulations. The company takes compliance seriously and has implemented several measures to ensure it adheres to local laws and international standards. These measures include:

- **Know Your [Customer](../c/customer.md) (KYC)**: A rigorous KYC process to verify user identities and prevent [fraud](../f/fraud.md).
- **Anti-[Money Laundering](../m/money_laundering.md) (AML)**: Comprehensive AML protocols to detect and report suspicious activities.
- **Data protection**: Compliance with laws such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) to protect user data.

## Institutional Services

To address the growing [demand](../d/demand.md) from institutional investors, Korbit has rolled out several services tailored to the needs of large financial entities. These include:

- **[Over-the-counter (OTC) trading](../o/over-the-counter_(otc)_trading.md)**: Facilitates large-[volume](../v/volume.md) trades with personalized service and more favorable rates.
- **Custodial services**: Secure storage solutions for institutional investors with large [asset](../a/asset.md) [holdings](../h/holdings.md).
- **API access**: Advanced trading API for programmatic access to the [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md), suitable for [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) and high-frequency [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).

## Technological Infrastructure

Korbit's platform is built on a [robust](../r/robust.md) technological [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md) designed to ensure reliability, [security](../s/security.md), and [scalability](../s/scalability.md). Some of the critical components include:

- **High-performance trading engine**: Capable of handling a large number of transactions per second, ensuring minimal latency.
- **[Security](../s/security.md) protocols**: Multi-layered [security](../s/security.md) measures, including encryption, [anomaly detection](../a/anomaly_detection.md), and regular [security](../s/security.md) audits.
- **[Scalability](../s/scalability.md)**: Designed to [handle](../h/handle.md) increasing user numbers and [transaction](../t/transaction.md) volumes without compromising performance.
