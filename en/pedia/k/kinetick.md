# Kinetick

## Overview
Kinetick is a [market](../m/market.md) data service provider that offers real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data for traders and investors. Designed to integrate seamlessly with the [NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md) [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md), Kinetick provides fast, reliable, and affordable [market](../m/market.md) data across a wide [range](../r/range.md) of [asset](../a/asset.md) classes, including [stocks](../s/stock.md), [futures](../f/futures.md), forex, and [options](../o/options.md).

## Features
1. **[Real-Time Market Data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Provides streaming [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md) with low latency, essential for active trading.
2. **Historical Data**: Offers extensive historical data for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) and analysis, including [tick](../t/tick.md), minute, and daily data.
3. **[Market Depth](../m/market_depth.md)**: Access to Level II [market](../m/market.md) data, providing detailed [order book](../o/order_book.md) information for a comprehensive view of [market depth](../m/market_depth.md).
4. **Wide Coverage**: Covers a broad [range](../r/range.md) of [asset](../a/asset.md) classes, including equities, [futures](../f/futures.md), forex, and [options](../o/options.md).
5. **News and Events**: Provides real-time news feeds and economic event data to keep traders informed of [market](../m/market.md)-moving events.
6. **Seamless Integration**: Designed to integrate seamlessly with [NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md) and other trading platforms for a smooth user experience.
7. **Customizable Data Feeds**: Users can tailor data feeds to their specific needs, choosing only the markets and data types they require.
8. **Affordability**: Competitive pricing plans designed to provide high-quality [market](../m/market.md) data at an affordable cost.

## Key Components
- **Real-Time Data Feed**: Delivers [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md) with low latency for various financial instruments.
- **Historical Data Services**: Provides historical data for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) and [market](../m/market.md) analysis, available in [multiple](../m/multiple.md) granularities.
- **[Market Depth](../m/market_depth.md) (Level II)**: Offers Level II data, showing the full depth of [market](../m/market.md) orders beyond the best [bid and ask](../b/bid_and_ask.md) prices.
- **News Feeds**: Real-time news and economic events data integrated into the platform for timely [market](../m/market.md) insights.
- **Data Management Tools**: Tools for managing, storing, and retrieving [market](../m/market.md) data efficiently.

## Integrations
Kinetick is designed to work seamlessly with [NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md), enhancing its functionality with reliable [market](../m/market.md) data. Some notable integrations include:

- **[NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md)**: Primary integration providing real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data directly within the [NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md) platform.
- **Third-Party Platforms**: Compatibility with various third-party trading platforms through API access, allowing users to integrate Kinetick data with their preferred trading tools.
- **[Brokerage Services](../b/brokerage_services.md)**: Integration with [multiple](../m/multiple.md) brokers for a unified trading and data analysis experience.
