# Kelley School of Business

The Kelley School of [Business](../b/business.md), located at Indiana University, Bloomington, is one of the premier [business](../b/business.md) schools in the United States. Known for its rigorous academic programs, innovative curriculum, and extensive alumni network, Kelley offers various undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs that focus on developing future [business](../b/business.md) leaders.

## History and Background
The Kelley School of [Business](../b/business.md) was established in 1920 as the Indiana University School of [Commerce](../c/commerce.md) and [Finance](../f/finance.md). Over the years, it evolved and expanded its academic offerings, and in 1997, it was renamed after E.W. Kelley, an IU alumnus and successful businessman, following his significant [endowment](../e/endowment.md).

## Academic Programs
Kelley School of [Business](../b/business.md) offers a comprehensive [range](../r/range.md) of academic programs catering to diverse interests in the [business](../b/business.md) field. These include:
- **Undergraduate Program**: Known for its highly competitive Bachelor of Science in [Business](../b/business.md) (BSB) degree, it offers majors in [accounting](../a/accounting.md), [finance](../f/finance.md), [marketing](../m/marketing.md), operations, and many more.
- **MBA Program**: The full-time MBA program at Kelley is renowned for its personalized approach, [leadership](../l/leadership.md) development, and global experiences.
- **Online MBA & MS Programs**: Through its Kelley Direct programs, the school provides high-quality online MBA and specialized masterâ€™s degrees, making education accessible to working professionals.
- **Doctoral Programs**: Covering areas like [Accounting](../a/accounting.md), [Finance](../f/finance.md), and [Marketing](../m/marketing.md), the PhD programs prepare students for research and academic careers.

## Research and Centers
The Kelley School of [Business](../b/business.md) is [home](../h/home.md) to various research centers and institutes that promote cutting-edge research and practical solutions in the [business](../b/business.md) world. Key centers include:
- **Institute for [Business](../b/business.md) Analytics**: Focuses on the application of [data analytics](../d/data_analytics.md) to [business](../b/business.md) problems.
- **Center for Brand [Leadership](../l/leadership.md)**: Specializes in the study and practice of [brand management](../b/brand_management.md).
- **Johnson Center for Entrepreneurship & Innovation**: Supports entrepreneurial education and ventures.

## Faculty and Their Contributions
The faculty at Kelley School of [Business](../b/business.md) consists of distinguished scholars and practitioners who contribute significantly to their fields. They are involved in research, publications, and consulting, which enriches the learning experience for students. Faculty members like Dr. Ramesh Venkataraman and Dr. Idie Kesner have made significant contributions to [finance](../f/finance.md) and management research, respectively.

## Global Education and Partnerships
Kelley has established numerous international partnerships with leading [business](../b/business.md) schools and universities worldwide. These collaborations facilitate student exchanges, dual degree programs, and [joint](../j/joint.md) research initiatives, [offering](../o/offering.md) a global perspective to its students.

## Career Services and Alumni Network
The Kelley School of [Business](../b/business.md) boasts a [robust](../r/robust.md) career services team that assists students in securing internships and job placements. Their global alumni network, known as Kelley Alumni Association, includes over 100,000 alumni, many of whom [hold](../h/hold.md) prominent positions in leading companies.
