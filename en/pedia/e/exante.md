# Exante

## Overview
Exante is a global [investment company](../i/investment_company.md) that offers direct [market](../m/market.md) access (DMA) to a wide [range](../r/range.md) of financial instruments through a single multi-[currency](../c/currency.md) account. Known for its sophisticated [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) and extensive [market](../m/market.md) coverage, Exante caters to both individual and institutional investors, providing them with advanced tools and competitive pricing.

## Features
1. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to over 50,000 financial instruments, including [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), [futures](../f/futures.md), forex, bonds, metals, and cryptocurrencies.
2. **Advanced [Trading Platform](../t/trading_platform.md)**: [Proprietary trading](../p/proprietary_trading.md) platform with powerful charting tools, [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md), and a user-friendly interface.
3. **Multi-[Currency](../c/currency.md) Accounts**: [Trade](../t/trade.md) in [multiple](../m/multiple.md) currencies from a single account, with seamless [currency](../c/currency.md) conversion.
4. **Direct [Market](../m/market.md) Access (DMA)**: Direct access to major global exchanges, ensuring fast and transparent [order](../o/order.md) [execution](../e/execution.md).
5. **Low Latency**: High-speed trading [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md) with low latency for efficient trading.
6. **Comprehensive Research Tools**: Access to in-depth [market](../m/market.md) analysis, news, and research reports.
7. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile app for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
8. **Automated Trading**: Supports automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) and [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md).
9. **Secure and Regulated**: Exante is regulated by [multiple](../m/multiple.md) financial authorities, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).
10. **[Customer](../c/customer.md) Support**: Multilingual [customer](../c/customer.md) support available to assist clients with their trading needs.
