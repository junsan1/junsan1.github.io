# Estimize

## Overview
Estimize is a financial data platform that crowdsources [earnings](../e/earnings.md) and economic estimates from a diverse community of analysts, including [buy-side](../b/buy-side.md) and [sell-side](../s/sell-side.md) analysts, [fund](../f/fund.md) managers, academics, and individual investors. Estimize aims to provide a more accurate and timely consensus on [earnings](../e/earnings.md) and economic forecasts compared to traditional [Wall Street](../w/wall_street.md) estimates.

## Features
1. **Crowdsourced Estimates**: Aggregates [earnings](../e/earnings.md) and economic forecasts from a wide [range](../r/range.md) of contributors, providing a comprehensive view of [market](../m/market.md) expectations.
2. **[Earnings Estimates](../e/earnings_estimate.md)**: Detailed [earnings](../e/earnings.md) per share (EPS) and [revenue](../r/revenue.md) estimates for thousands of publicly traded companies.
3. **[Economic Indicators](../e/economic_indicators.md)**: Forecasts for major [economic indicators](../e/economic_indicators.md) such as GDP, [unemployment](../u/unemployment.md) rates, and non-farm payrolls.
4. **Consensus Data**: Provides a consensus estimate that reflects the average of all submitted estimates.
5. **Historical Accuracy**: Tracks the historical accuracy of individual contributors and the overall consensus, helping users identify the most reliable sources.
6. **[Data Integration](../d/data_integration.md)**: Integration with various financial platforms and tools for seamless data access and analysis.
7. **Visualization Tools**: Advanced [data visualization](../d/data_visualization.md) tools to analyze trends, compare estimates, and track [forecast accuracy](../f/forecast_accuracy.md).
8. **API Access**: API for programmatic access to Estimize data, enabling integration with custom applications and [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
9. **Community Insights**: Insights and analysis from the Estimize community, [offering](../o/offering.md) diverse perspectives on [market](../m/market.md) expectations.
10. **Alerts and Notifications**: Customizable alerts and notifications for updates on [earnings estimates](../e/earnings_estimate.md) and economic forecasts.

## Key Components
- **[Earnings Estimates](../e/earnings_estimate.md)**: Aggregated [earnings](../e/earnings.md) per share (EPS) and [revenue](../r/revenue.md) forecasts for a wide [range](../r/range.md) of companies.
- **Economic Forecasts**: Predictions for key [economic indicators](../e/economic_indicators.md), contributed by a diverse group of analysts.
- **Consensus Estimates**: Average of all submitted estimates, providing a comprehensive [market](../m/market.md) expectation.
- **Historical Data**: Access to historical estimate data for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) and analysis.
- **Contributor Rankings**: Tracks and ranks contributors based on the accuracy of their forecasts.
- **[Data Visualization](../d/data_visualization.md)**: Tools for visualizing estimate trends and comparing forecasts across different time periods.
- **API**: Programmatic access to Estimize data for integration with trading platforms and analytical tools.

## Integrations
Estimize integrates with various financial platforms, data providers, and analytical tools to enhance its usability. Some notable integrations include:

- **Trading Platforms**: Compatibility with trading platforms like [Bloomberg](../b/bloomberg.md), [Reuters](../r/reuters.md), and others for seamless data access.
- **[Financial Analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md) Tools**: Integration with tools such as Microsoft Excel, MATLAB, and R for advanced analysis and visualization.
- **APIs**: Provides APIs for custom integrations and automated data retrieval.
- **Third-Party Applications**: Support for various third-party financial applications and services to extend functionality.
