# Sierra Chart

Sierra Chart is a highly sophisticated, professional-grade trading and charting software platform designed for serious traders, financial analysts, and institutions. Renowned for its robustness, flexibility, and extensive feature set, Sierra Chart has established itself as a go-to solution for those requiring advanced trading capabilities across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [asset](../a/asset.md) classes.

## Core Functionality

### 1. Charting and Technical Analysis

Sierra Chart's charting capabilities are among the most advanced in the [industry](../i/industry.md):

- **Chart Types**: Offers a wide array of chart types including [candlestick](../c/candlestick.md), bar, line, point & figure, Renko, Kagi, and more.
- **Time Frames**: Supports custom time frames from [tick](../t/tick.md)-by-[tick](../t/tick.md) to yearly charts, with the ability to create non-standard intervals.
- **Multi-Symbol [Overlay](../o/overlay.md)**: Allows for the comparison of [multiple](../m/multiple.md) symbols on a single chart, essential for [correlation analysis](../c/correlation_analysis.md).
- **Drawing Tools**: Extensive set of drawing tools including [trend](../t/trend.md) lines, Fibonacci retracements, [Elliott Wave patterns](../e/elliott_wave_patterns.md), and custom shapes.
- **Indicators**: Features over 300 built-in [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md), covering everything from simple moving averages to complex algorithmic studies.
- **Custom Indicators**: Allows for the creation of custom indicators using C++ or ACSIL (Advanced Custom Study Interface Language), providing unlimited analytical possibilities.
- **Real-Time Updates**: All charts and studies update in real-time, ensuring traders always have the latest [market](../m/market.md) information.

### 2. Order Execution and Trading

Sierra Chart provides a comprehensive trading interface:

- **[Order Types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md)**: Supports a wide [range](../r/range.md) of [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md) including [market](../m/market.md), limit, stop, OCO (One-Cancels-Other), trailing stops, and more.
- **Direct [Market](../m/market.md) Access (DMA)**: Offers direct connections to numerous exchanges, allowing for faster [execution](../e/execution.md) and reduced [slippage](../s/slippage.md).
- **DOM Trading**: Features a dynamic Depth of [Market](../m/market.md) (DOM) window for rapid [order](../o/order.md) placement and [market depth analysis](../m/market_depth_analysis.md).
- **[Position Management](../p/position_management.md)**: Real-time tracking of positions, P&L, and [risk metrics](../r/risk_metrics.md) across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) accounts and instruments.
- **Multi-[Broker](../b/broker.md) Support**: Integrates with various FCMs and brokers, allowing traders to use their preferred [execution](../e/execution.md) services.

### 3. Automated Trading

The platform offers [robust](../r/robust.md) capabilities for automated and [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md):

- **Strategy Development**: Traders can create complex [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) using C++ or ACSIL.
- **[Backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) Engine**: Comprehensive [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md) capabilities allow for strategy [optimization](../o/optimization.md) and historical performance analysis.
- **Real-Time Strategy [Execution](../e/execution.md)**: Automated strategies can be executed in real-time [market](../m/market.md) conditions.
- **Event-Driven Architecture**: The system responds to [market](../m/market.md) events in real-time, allowing for high-frequency trading implementations.

### 4. Data Management and Analysis

Sierra Chart excels in handling and analyzing [market](../m/market.md) data:

- **[Multiple](../m/multiple.md) Data Feeds**: Integrates with numerous data providers for both real-time and historical data across various [asset](../a/asset.md) classes.
- **Data Recording**: Ability to record and store [tick](../t/tick.md)-by-[tick](../t/tick.md) data for later analysis or [market](../m/market.md) replay.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Replay**: Sophisticated [market](../m/market.md) replay functionality allows traders to review historical [market](../m/market.md) conditions in detail.
- **[Volume Analysis](../v/volume_analysis.md)**: Advanced [volume profile](../v/volume_profile.md) and [market](../m/market.md) profile tools for in-depth [market structure analysis](../m/market_structure_analysis.md).
- **Time and Sales**: Detailed time and sales window for [transaction](../t/transaction.md)-level analysis.

### 5. Risk Management

The platform includes several features for effective [risk management](../r/risk_management.md):

- **[Position Sizing](../p/position_sizing.md)**: Tools for calculating and implementing appropriate position sizes based on [account balance](../a/account_balance.md) and [risk tolerance](../r/risk_tolerance.md).
- **Stop Loss Management**: Automated stop loss placement and [trailing stop](../t/trailing_stop.md) capabilities.
- **[Margin](../m/margin.md) Calculation**: Real-time [margin](../m/margin.md) requirement calculations to prevent over-leveraging.
- **[Scenario Analysis](../s/scenario_analysis.md)**: Tools for analyzing potential outcomes of trades under various [market](../m/market.md) conditions.

## Technical Specifications

- **Performance**: Known for its high-speed performance, capable of handling large amounts of data and complex calculations with minimal latency.
- **Stability**: [Robust](../r/robust.md) architecture ensures stability even under high-stress [market](../m/market.md) conditions.
- **Customization**: Nearly every aspect of the platform can be customized, from chart layouts to keyboard shortcuts.
- **API Integration**: Offers APIs for integration with external systems and data sources.
- **Multi-Monitor Support**: Designed to work seamlessly across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) monitors for an expansive trading setup.

## Platform Compatibility and Requirements

- **Operating Systems**: 
  - Windows 7, 8, 10, 11 (32-bit and 64-bit versions)
  - Linux (via Wine)
- **Hardware Requirements**:
  - Minimum: Dual-core processor, 4GB RAM, 1GB free disk space
  - Recommended: Quad-core processor or better, 8GB+ RAM, SSD for improved performance
