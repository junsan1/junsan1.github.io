# SelfWealth

## Overview
SelfWealth is an Australian online brokerage platform that offers a cost-effective and user-friendly trading experience. Known for its low-cost trading and innovative features, SelfWealth provides access to Australian and US equities, ETFs, and other investment products. The platform is designed to cater to individual investors, offering a range of tools and resources to support informed investment decisions.

## Features
1. **Low-Cost Trading**: Flat-fee trading with no hidden costs or commissions.
2. **Wide Range of Instruments**: Access to Australian and US equities, ETFs, and other investment products.
3. **Advanced Trading Platform**: A user-friendly web platform with advanced charting tools, real-time data, and market analysis.
4. **Community Insights**: Social trading features that allow users to compare their portfolio performance with other investors.
5. **Research and Analysis**: Comprehensive research reports, market news, and analysis tools.
6. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including articles, tutorials, and market analysis to help traders improve their skills.
7. **Portfolio Management**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
8. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile app for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
9. **Secure and Regulated**: SelfWealth is regulated by top-tier financial authorities in Australia, ensuring a secure trading environment.
10. **Customer Support**: Dedicated customer support available to assist clients with their trading and investment needs.
