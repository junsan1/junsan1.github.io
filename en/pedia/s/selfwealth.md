# SelfWealth

## Overview
SelfWealth is an Australian online brokerage platform that offers a cost-effective and user-friendly trading experience. Known for its low-cost trading and innovative features, SelfWealth provides access to Australian and US equities, ETFs, and other investment products. The platform is designed to cater to individual investors, [offering](../o/offering.md) a [range](../r/range.md) of tools and resources to support informed investment decisions.

## Features
1. **Low-Cost Trading**: Flat-[fee](../f/fee.md) trading with no hidden costs or commissions.
2. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to Australian and US equities, ETFs, and other investment products.
3. **Advanced [Trading Platform](../t/trading_platform.md)**: A user-friendly web platform with advanced charting tools, real-time data, and [market](../m/market.md) analysis.
4. **Community Insights**: [Social trading](../s/social_trading.md) features that allow users to compare their [portfolio performance](../p/portfolio_performance.md) with other investors.
5. **Research and Analysis**: Comprehensive research reports, [market](../m/market.md) news, and analysis tools.
6. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including articles, tutorials, and [market](../m/market.md) analysis to help traders improve their skills.
7. **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md)**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
8. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile app for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
9. **Secure and Regulated**: SelfWealth is regulated by top-tier financial authorities in Australia, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).
10. **[Customer](../c/customer.md) Support**: Dedicated [customer](../c/customer.md) support available to assist clients with their trading and investment needs.
