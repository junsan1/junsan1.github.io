# Saxo Bank

## Overview
Saxo [Bank](../b/bank.md) is a leading online trading and investment specialist, [offering](../o/offering.md) a comprehensive [range](../r/range.md) of financial services to individual investors, traders, and institutional clients. With its advanced trading platforms and wide [range](../r/range.md) of tradable instruments, Saxo [Bank](../b/bank.md) provides access to global [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md), including forex, [stocks](../s/stock.md), bonds, commodities, and [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md).

## Features
1. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to forex, [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, bonds, commodities, [options](../o/options.md), [futures](../f/futures.md), and CFDs.
2. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers [multiple](../m/multiple.md) trading platforms including SaxoTraderGO, SaxoTraderPRO, and mobile apps with advanced charting tools and real-time data.
3. **Competitive Pricing**: Low commissions and [spreads](../s/spreads.md) with transparent pricing structures.
4. **[Market Research](../m/market_research.md) and Analysis**: Access to comprehensive research reports, [market](../m/market.md) news, and advanced analysis tools.
5. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including webinars, tutorials, articles, and [market](../m/market.md) analysis to help traders improve their skills.
6. **Automated Trading**: Supports automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) and [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) through APIs and third-party integrations.
7. **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md) Tools**: Includes advanced [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md), [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) tools, and real-time [risk metrics](../r/risk_metrics.md).
8. **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md)**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
9. **Multi-[Currency](../c/currency.md) Accounts**: [Trade](../t/trade.md) and [hold](../h/hold.md) [multiple](../m/multiple.md) currencies within a single account.
10. **Secure and Regulated**: Saxo [Bank](../b/bank.md) is regulated by top-tier financial authorities, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).

## Key Components
- **SaxoTraderGO**: A user-friendly [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) [offering](../o/offering.md) advanced charting, real-time data, and a [range](../r/range.md) of trading tools.
- **SaxoTraderPRO**: An advanced [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) designed for professional traders, providing [robust](../r/robust.md) charting tools, [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md), and customizable layouts.
- **Mobile Trading Apps**: Mobile applications for iOS and Android with full trading functionality.
- **[Market Research](../m/market_research.md) and Analysis Tools**: Comprehensive tools for [market research](../m/market_research.md), [stock analysis](../s/stock_analysis.md), and investment insights.
- **Educational Resources**: A library of educational content, including webinars, tutorials, and articles.
- **API Access**: APIs for automated trading and custom integrations.

## Integrations
Saxo [Bank](../b/bank.md) integrates with a variety of financial platforms and tools to enhance its trading services. Some notable integrations include:

- **[Market](../m/market.md) Data Providers**: Integration with leading [market](../m/market.md) data providers for real-time and historical data.
- **[TradingView](../t/tradingview.md)**: Integration with [TradingView](../t/tradingview.md) for advanced charting and [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md).

- **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md) Tools**: Integration with [portfolio management](../p/portfolio_management.md) and tracking tools.

## Use Cases
- **Individual Investors**: Utilized by individual investors for managing their investments, accessing research, and trading in global markets.
- **Active Traders**: Provides active traders with powerful trading tools, [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md), and advanced analysis.
- **Institutional Clients**: Offers tailored solutions for institutional clients, including [hedge](../h/hedge.md) funds, [asset](../a/asset.md) managers, and [proprietary trading](../p/proprietary_trading.md) firms.
- **Algorithmic Traders**: Supports algorithmic traders in developing and deploying custom [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) through APIs and trading platforms.
- **Educational Purposes**: Supports new and experienced traders with extensive educational resources and [market](../m/market.md) analysis.
- **Portfolio Managers**: Assists portfolio managers in tracking and managing their investment portfolios with comprehensive tools and reports.

## Regulation
Saxo [Bank](../b/bank.md) is regulated by several top-tier financial authorities, ensuring a secure and compliant [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md):
- Danish Financial Supervisory Authority (FSA)
- Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) in the UK
- Australian Securities and Investments [Commission](../c/commission.md) (ASIC)
- Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS)
- Swiss Financial [Market](../m/market.md) Supervisory Authority (FINMA)
