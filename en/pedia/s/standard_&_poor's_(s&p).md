# Standard & Poor's (S&P)

Standard & Poor's (S&P) is a financial services company that provides a [range](../r/range.md) of products and services in the areas of [credit](../c/credit.md) ratings, research, and analytics. Established in 1860, S&P is one of the oldest and most influential firms in [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md), shaping investment decisions, economic policies, and [risk](../r/risk.md) assessments worldwide. S&P is most well-known for its [stock market](../s/stock_market.md) indices, such as the S&P 500, and its widely-used [credit](../c/credit.md) ratings.

## Credit Ratings

One of the core services offered by S&P is [credit rating](../c/credit_rating.md). [Credit](../c/credit.md) ratings assess the [creditworthiness](../c/creditworthiness.md) of borrowers, including corporations, governments, and financial instruments. Ratings are divided into two main categories: [investment grade](../i/investment_grade.md) and speculative grade (or junk). They [range](../r/range.md) from '[AAA](../a/aaa.md)' (highest quality) to 'D' ([default](../d/default.md)).

### Importance of Credit Ratings

1. **Investment Decision-Making:** Investors use S&Pâ€™s ratings to determine the [risk](../r/risk.md) level of potential investments.
2. **Cost of Borrowing:** Higher-rated entities are usually able to borrow at lower [interest](../i/interest.md) rates.
3. **[Market](../m/market.md) Stability:** Ratings contribute to [market](../m/market.md) [transparency](../t/transparency.md) and stability by providing consistent criteria for evaluating [risk](../r/risk.md).

### Process

S&P follows a rigorous procedure for issuing [credit](../c/credit.md) ratings:
1. **Data Collection:** Financial data, operational history, and economic environment are gathered.
2. **Analysis:** A team of analysts rigorously evaluates the data against established criteria.
3. **Committee Review:** A [rating](../r/rating.md) committee reviews the analysis to ensure objectivity and accuracy.
4. **Publication:** Ratings are published and monitored continuously.

## S&P Indices

S&P is also renowned for its broad array of [market](../m/market.md) indices. One of the most famous is the S&P 500, which tracks the performance of 500 of the largest [listed](../l/listed.md) companies in the U.S.

### S&P 500

The S&P 500 is a [benchmark](../b/benchmark.md) [index](../i/index_instrument.md) widely regarded as one of the best representations of the U.S. [stock market](../s/stock_market.md). It includes companies from [multiple](../m/multiple.md) sectors, which provides a comprehensive snapshot of [market](../m/market.md) trends and economic health.

### Other Key Indices

- **S&P Global 1200:** A comprehensive, seven-country [index](../i/index_instrument.md) that covers major global markets.
- **S&P MidCap 400:** Focuses on mid-sized companies.
- **S&P SmallCap 600:** Targets smaller enterprises.

## S&P Global

S&P operates under the umbrella of S&P Global, a [corporation](../c/corporation.md) that provides essential intelligence for companies, governments, and individuals to make decisions with conviction.

### Divisions

1. **S&P Global Ratings:** The [business](../b/business.md) unit responsible for issuing [credit](../c/credit.md) ratings.
2. **[S&P Global Market Intelligence](../s/snp_global_market_intelligence.md):** Provides data and analytical insights.
3. **S&P Dow Jones Indices:** Manages and licenses [market](../m/market.md) indices.
4. **S&P Global Platts:** Specializes in energy and commodities information.

Visit [S&P Global](https://www.spglobal.com/) for more information.

## Analytical Services

S&P also offers a variety of analytical services that provide insights into [market](../m/market.md) trends, [economic conditions](../e/economic_conditions.md), and sectoral performance.

### Portfolio Analytics

For investment managers, S&P offers portfolio analytics tools that help in [risk](../r/risk.md) assessment, [performance attribution](../p/performance_attribution.md), and strategic planning.

### Economic Research

S&P produces in-depth reports and forecasts on global [economic conditions](../e/economic_conditions.md), including GDP [growth rates](../g/growth_rates_in_trading.md), [inflation](../i/inflation.md) trends, and fiscal policies.

### Market Data

S&P provides extensive [market](../m/market.md) data services, including real-time and historical data across various [asset](../a/asset.md) classes.

## Technological Integration

In recent years, S&P has integrated advanced technologies like AI and [Big Data analytics](../b/big_data_analytics_in_trading.md) to enhance its offerings. This ensures that the insights it provides are timely, accurate, and actionable.

### Algotrading

For [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md), S&P provides datasets that can be integrated into [trading algorithms](../t/trading_algorithms.md) to optimize investment strategies. [Data analytics](../d/data_analytics.md) platforms can use S&P's data to create [predictive models](../p/predictive_models_in_trading.md), back-test strategies, and set automated trading parameters.

### Fintech Collaborations

S&P collaborates with fintech companies to develop innovative financial products. These partnerships often result in tools that [offer](../o/offer.md) real-time analytics, enhanced [risk management](../r/risk_management.md), and automated compliance solutions.

## Conclusion

Standard & Poor's plays a pivotal role in the global financial ecosystem. Its [credit](../c/credit.md) ratings, [market](../m/market.md) indices, and analytical services are indispensable tools for investors, policymakers, and financial professionals. By continually evolving and integrating new technologies, S&P maintains its status as one of the most influential entities in [finance](../f/finance.md).
