# Jim Walton

Jim Walton is a prominent American businessman known not only for his immense [wealth](../w/wealth.md) but also for his significant influence in various sectors, primarily through his association with Walmart, the multinational retail giant. As the youngest son of Walmart founder Sam Walton, Jim Walton has played a crucial role in the family [business](../b/business.md) and has expanded his reach into banking and [philanthropy](../p/philanthropy.md).

## Early Life and Education

Jim Walton was born on June 7, 1948, in Newport, Arkansas. He is the third and youngest son of Sam Walton and Helen Walton. He grew up immersed in the retail environment due to his father's [business](../b/business.md) and later attended the University of Arkansas, earning a degree in [Business](../b/business.md) Administration and [Marketing](../m/marketing.md) in 1971.

## Role in Walmart

Jim Walton joined the family [business](../b/business.md) early in his career, taking on various roles that enabled him to [gain](../g/gain.md) a comprehensive understanding of Walmart's operations. He served on Walmart's board of directors for several years, during which the company experienced tremendous growth, becoming the world's largest retail chain.

## Arvest Bank

One of Jim Walton's most significant achievements outside of Walmart is his involvement with Arvest [Bank](../b/bank.md). Founded in 1961 and primarily owned by the Walton family, Arvest [Bank](../b/bank.md) has grown under Jim's [leadership](../l/leadership.md) to become one of the largest banks in the Midwest. He served as the chairman of the board for Arvest [Bank](../b/bank.md) until 2016, playing a crucial role in its [expansion](../e/expansion.md) and modernization efforts.

- [Arvest Bank](https://www.arvest.com/)

## Philanthropy

Jim Walton, like other members of the Walton family, is actively involved in [philanthropy](../p/philanthropy.md). He is a [trustee](../t/trustee.md) of the Walton Family Foundation, which focuses on education, community development, and environmental conservation. The foundation has donated hundreds of millions of dollars to various causes, aiming to create long-term positive impacts.

- [Walton Family Foundation](https://www.waltonfamilyfoundation.org/)

## Real Estate and Other Investments

Beyond banking and retail, Jim Walton has diversified his investment portfolio. He has invested in various [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) projects and other [business](../b/business.md) ventures, further increasing his [wealth](../w/wealth.md) and influence in the [business](../b/business.md) world.

## Personal Life

Jim Walton is known for maintaining a relatively low profile compared to other billionaires. He resides in Bentonville, Arkansas, with his family. Despite his immense [wealth](../w/wealth.md), he is known to lead a modest lifestyle.

## Net Worth

As of the latest estimates, Jim Walton's [net worth](../n/net_worth.md) is approximately $70 billion, making him one of the richest individuals in the world. His [wealth](../w/wealth.md) is primarily derived from his [shares](../s/shares.md) in Walmart, as well as his investments in Arvest [Bank](../b/bank.md) and other ventures.

## Conclusion

Jim Walton's influence spans across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) sectors, from retail and banking to [philanthropy](../p/philanthropy.md). As a key figure in the Walton family, he continues to shape the legacy of Walmart while also making significant contributions to society through his philanthropic efforts. His diverse career and substantial impact make him a unique and influential figure in the [business](../b/business.md) world.
