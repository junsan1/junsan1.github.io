# Judge Business School

Judge [Business](../b/business.md) School, officially known as Cambridge Judge [Business](../b/business.md) School (CJBS), is the [business](../b/business.md) school of the University of Cambridge. It was established in 1990 and named after Sir Paul Judge, who was one of the founding benefactors. The school is located on the site of the Old Addenbrooke's Site on Trumpington Street in central Cambridge. Emphasizing a collaborative approach to [business](../b/business.md) education, Cambridge Judge [Business](../b/business.md) School integrates inter-disciplinary techniques to advance knowledge in core areas such as entrepreneurial studies, [finance](../f/finance.md), and [operations management](../o/operations_management.md), to name a few.

The mission of Cambridge Judge [Business](../b/business.md) School is to [leverage](../l/leverage.md) the power of academia for real-world problem solving. It aims to educate and prepare individuals in the various facets of [business](../b/business.md) management with a forward-looking perspective.

## Academic Programs
CJBS offers a variety of programs ranging from undergraduate studies to executive education. 

### MBA Program
The MBA at Cambridge Judge is an intensive one-year program. It incorporates a mix of lectures, workshops, group discussions, and projects. It also emphasizes practical experience through consulting projects and a global consulting project.

Details about the program can be found at: [Cambridge MBA](https://www.jbs.cam.ac.uk/programmes/mba/)

### Executive MBA
The Executive MBA (EMBA) is geared towards experienced professionals who wish to continue working while earning their degree. The program lasts around 20 months and includes rigorous coursework and a final project. 

For more information: [Cambridge Executive MBA](https://www.jbs.cam.ac.uk/programmes/executive-mba/)

### Master of Finance (MFin)
Targeted at professionals who aim to advance their [finance](../f/finance.md) careers, the MFin program provides specialized [finance](../f/finance.md) knowledge. This one-year course focuses on [finance](../f/finance.md) theory, practice, and real-world applications.

Further details: [Master of Finance](https://www.jbs.cam.ac.uk/programmes/master-of-finance/)

### PhD Program
CJBS also offers a doctoral program which focuses on producing leading research scholars. The program accommodates students interested in various disciplines within the [business](../b/business.md) and management field.

To explore more: [PhD in Management](https://www.jbs.cam.ac.uk/programmes/research-programmes/phd/)

## Research Centers and Institutes

### Centre for Business Research (CBR)
The Centre for [Business](../b/business.md) Research conducts interdisciplinary research in areas like employment change, economic performance, and innovation policy. It aims to support policy-making through rigorous [empirical analysis](../e/empirical_analysis_in_trading.md).

More information: [Centre for Business Research](https://www.cbr.cam.ac.uk/)

### Centre for Financial Analysis & Policy (CFAP)
CFAP focuses on the [financial sector](../f/financial_sector.md), from [asset](../a/asset.md) pricing and [corporate finance](../c/corporate_finance.md) to [risk management](../r/risk_management.md). It aims to influence policy and practice in [financial markets](../f/financial_market.md) and institutions.

Details: [Centre for Financial Analysis & Policy](https://www.jbs.cam.ac.uk/faculty-research/centres/cfap/)

### Centre for Risk Studies (CRS)
CRS at CJBS investigates risks that could potentially cause societal disruptions. This center aims to provide [risk management frameworks](../r/risk_management_frameworks.md) that firms and governments can utilize.

Learn more: [Centre for Risk Studies](https://www.jbs.cam.ac.uk/faculty-research/centres/risk/)
