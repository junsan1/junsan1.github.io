# Johnson School at Cornell University

The Samuel Curtis Johnson Graduate School of Management, commonly known as the Johnson School, is the graduate [business](../b/business.md) school of Cornell University, a member of the Ivy League. Situated in Ithaca, New York, the school offers a [range](../r/range.md) of [business](../b/business.md) programs, catering to individuals seeking advanced academic knowledge and strategic managerial skills. One distinguishing [factor](../f/factor.md) of the Johnson School is its emphasis on [leadership](../l/leadership.md), innovation, and sustainability, aligning with Cornell University’s broader mission of public engagement and societal impact.

## History and Establishment

The Johnson School was established in 1946 and has since grown in reputation and size. The school was renamed in 1984 after Samuel Curtis Johnson, Sr., a prominent [business](../b/business.md) leader and philanthropist, following a substantial [endowment](../e/endowment.md) from the Johnson family. This [endowment](../e/endowment.md) has allowed the school to expand its faculty, facilities, and program offerings.

## Academic Programs

The Johnson School offers a variety of programs designed to meet the needs of a diverse student body:

### MBA Programs

1. **Two-Year MBA**: This is the flagship program of the Johnson School. It provides a comprehensive curriculum that combines core [business](../b/business.md) principles with electives, allowing students to tailor their education to their career goals. Students typically engage in team-based projects, case studies, and real-world problem solving.

2. **One-Year MBA**: Designed for professionals with an advanced degree or substantial work experience, this accelerated program focuses on core [business](../b/business.md) disciplines and [leadership](../l/leadership.md) skills, allowing students to [return](../r/return.md) to the workforce swiftly.

3. **Executive MBA Programs**: These programs are designed for working professionals who seek to advance their careers without taking significant time off. The Executive MBA Metro NY and the Executive MBA Americas are two prominent offerings, featuring weekend classes and global learning modules.

4. **Johnson Cornell Tech MBA**: This one-year program combines [business](../b/business.md) education with technology and entrepreneurship, conducted at Cornell Tech’s campus in New York City. It is designed for aspiring tech leaders and innovators.

### Other Graduate Degrees

1. **PhD in Management**: This program prepares students for academic careers, focusing on rigorous research in various disciplines like [finance](../f/finance.md), [marketing](../m/marketing.md), and organizational behavior.

2. **Master of Professional Studies (MPS) in Management**: Tailored for early-career professionals, this program provides advanced training in specific areas such as [accounting](../a/accounting.md), [business](../b/business.md) analytics, and management.

### Dual Degree Programs

The Johnson School also offers several dual degree programs in collaboration with other colleges at Cornell and external institutions:

1. **MBA/MILR (Master of Industrial and Labor Relations)**: Combining [business](../b/business.md) management with labor relations, this program equips students for roles in corporate human resources, labor unions, and government.

2. **MBA/MEng (Master of Engineering)**: Aimed at students who want to blend technical expertise with managerial skills.

3. **MBA/JD**: This program crosses [business](../b/business.md) administration with law, preparing students for careers in corporate law, compliance, and legal consulting.

## Curriculum and Learning Approach

The Johnson School's curriculum is grounded in a balanced mix of theory and practice. Key components include:

### Core Curriculum

The core curriculum covers foundational courses in [finance](../f/finance.md), [marketing](../m/marketing.md), operations, strategy, [leadership](../l/leadership.md), and [economics](../e/economics.md). These courses are required for all MBA students and are designed to build essential [business](../b/business.md) knowledge.

### Immersion Learning

A hallmark of the Johnson MBA program is its immersion learning component. Students engage in [industry](../i/industry.md)-specific projects and practicums, such as the [Investment Banking](../i/investment_banking.md) Immersion or the Sustainable Global Enterprise Immersion, providing real-world experience and [networking](../n/networking.md) opportunities.

### Electives and Concentrations

Students can choose from a [wide variety](../w/wide_variety.md) of electives, allowing them to specialize in areas like [asset management](../a/asset_management.md), entrepreneurship, digital technology, or healthcare. Concentrations help students develop expertise and enhance their career prospects.

### Leadership Development

[Leadership](../l/leadership.md) development is a critical aspect of the Johnson School experience. The school's [Leadership](../l/leadership.md) Programs, including the Johnson [Leadership](../l/leadership.md) Fellows and the Leading Teams Practicum, focus on building interpersonal and managerial skills.

## Faculty and Research

The Johnson School's faculty members are renowned for their expertise and innovation in various fields of [business](../b/business.md) management and [economics](../e/economics.md). Faculty engage in cutting-edge research, contributing to academic literature and [industry](../i/industry.md) practices. Research centers and initiatives at the school include:

1. **Parker Center for Investment Research**: Focuses on [asset management](../a/asset_management.md) and offers practical training through the Cayuga [Fund](../f/fund.md), a student-managed [investment fund](../i/investment_fund.md).

2. **Center for Sustainable Global Enterprise**: Promotes sustainability through research, education, and outreach.

3. **Smith Family [Business](../b/business.md) Initiative**: Supports family-owned businesses with research, education, and [networking](../n/networking.md) opportunities.

## Career Management and Alumni Network

The Johnson School’s Career Management Center provides comprehensive support to students and alumni, including career counseling, job placement services, and [networking](../n/networking.md) opportunities. The school's alumni network is strong and active, with graduates holding [leadership](../l/leadership.md) positions in various industries worldwide. Notable alumni include prominent [business](../b/business.md) leaders, entrepreneurs, and academics.

## Facilities and Resources

The Johnson School's main building, Sage Hall, features state-of-the-art classrooms, collaborative spaces, and research facilities. The school also benefits from Cornell University's extensive resources, including libraries, research centers, and collaborative programs.

## Global Reach and Partnerships

The Johnson School has a global presence through partnerships with international institutions and organizations. It offers [exchange](../e/exchange.md) programs, global study treks, and dual degree [options](../o/options.md) with universities abroad, enhancing the global perspective of its students.

## Conclusion

The Samuel Curtis Johnson Graduate School of Management at Cornell University provides a rigorous and dynamic [business](../b/business.md) education, preparing students to lead and innovate in a rapidly changing global [economy](../e/economy.md). With its strong academic programs, cutting-edge research, and extensive network, the Johnson School stands as a premier institution for aspiring [business](../b/business.md) leaders.
