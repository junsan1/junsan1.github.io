# Jigsaw Trading

## Overview
Jigsaw Trading is a trading software company that provides advanced tools and educational resources for [order](../o/order.md) flow trading. Their platform is designed to help traders understand and [leverage](../l/leverage.md) [order](../o/order.md) flow to make more informed trading decisions. Jigsaw Trading's tools are widely used by day traders and professional traders to [gain](../g/gain.md) a competitive edge in the markets.

## Features
1. **[Order Flow Analysis](../o/order_flow_analysis.md)**: Comprehensive tools for analyzing [order](../o/order.md) flow, including real-time depth of [market](../m/market.md) (DOM) and [volume profile](../v/volume_profile.md).
2. **Advanced DOM**: A highly interactive and customizable depth of [market](../m/market.md) interface to view [market depth](../m/market_depth.md) and [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md).
3. **[Volume Profile](../v/volume_profile.md)**: Detailed [volume profile](../v/volume_profile.md) analysis to identify [key support and resistance levels](../k/key_support_and_resistance_levels.md).
4. **[Trade](../t/trade.md) Management**: Tools for managing trades, including automated [trade](../t/trade.md) management and advanced [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md).
5. **Historical Analysis**: Ability to replay [market](../m/market.md) data to analyze past [order](../o/order.md) flow and improve [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
6. **Trading Simulator**: [Simulated trading](../s/simulated_trading.md) environment for practicing [order](../o/order.md) flow trading without risking real [money](../m/money.md).
7. **Integration**: Compatible with various trading platforms and brokers for seamless trading [execution](../e/execution.md).
8. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including webinars, courses, and a trading community to help traders improve their skills.

## Key Components
- **Jigsaw Daytradr**: The primary [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) [offering](../o/offering.md) advanced [order](../o/order.md) flow tools, real-time DOM, and [volume profile](../v/volume_profile.md) analysis.
- **Jigsaw Journalytix**: A [trade](../t/trade.md) analytics and journaling tool to help traders review and improve their [trading performance](../t/trading_performance.md).
- **Reconstructed Tape**: A tool that provides a detailed view of the tape, showing [order](../o/order.md) flow dynamics and [market sentiment](../m/market_sentiment.md).
- **[Trade](../t/trade.md) Simulator**: A powerful trading simulator that allows traders to practice and refine their strategies in a [risk](../r/risk.md)-free environment.
- **Educational Platform**: A comprehensive suite of educational resources designed to teach the principles of [order](../o/order.md) flow trading.
