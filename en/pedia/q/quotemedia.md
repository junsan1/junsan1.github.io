# QuoteMedia

## Overview
QuoteMedia is a leading provider of financial [market](../m/market.md) data, news, and analytics solutions. The company offers a comprehensive suite of services designed to deliver high-quality, real-time and historical data across various [asset](../a/asset.md) classes, including equities, [options](../o/options.md), mutual funds, bonds, and commodities. QuoteMedia's products cater to a wide [range](../r/range.md) of clients, including brokers, financial institutions, media outlets, and individual investors.

## Features
1. **[Real-Time Market Data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Provides fast and accurate real-time data for equities, [options](../o/options.md), mutual funds, bonds, and commodities.
2. **Historical Data**: Extensive historical data for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md), research, and analysis, including daily, intraday, and [tick](../t/tick.md)-level data.
3. **[Market](../m/market.md) News**: Access to real-time financial news and [market](../m/market.md)-moving events.
4. **Fundamental Data**: Detailed [financial statements](../f/financial_statements.md), ratios, and company profiles for comprehensive analysis.
5. **[Technical Analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md)**: Advanced charting tools and [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md) for in-depth [market](../m/market.md) analysis.
6. **Data Feeds and APIs**: [Robust](../r/robust.md) data feeds and APIs for seamless integration with trading platforms, financial applications, and websites.
7. **Research Tools**: Comprehensive research tools, including screeners, watchlists, and portfolio trackers.
8. **Custom Data Solutions**: Tailored data solutions to meet specific client needs and requirements.
9. **[Market](../m/market.md) Analytics**: Tools for [market sentiment analysis](../m/market_sentiment_analysis.md), [earnings estimates](../e/earnings_estimate.md), and analyst ratings.
10. **Mobile Access**: Mobile-friendly solutions for accessing data and analytics on the go.
