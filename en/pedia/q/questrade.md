# Questrade

## Overview
Questrade is a leading online brokerage in Canada, known for its low-cost trading, comprehensive trading platforms, and wide [range](../r/range.md) of investment products. The platform caters to individual investors and traders, [offering](../o/offering.md) services in [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), mutual funds, and more. Questrade is committed to providing innovative technology, valuable educational resources, and exceptional [customer service](../c/customer_service.md).

## Features
1. **Low-Cost Trading**: Competitive pricing with low commissions and transparent [fee](../f/fee.md) structures.
2. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), mutual funds, GICs, and bonds.
3. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers [multiple](../m/multiple.md) trading platforms including Questrade Trading, Questrade IQ Edge, and mobile apps with advanced charting tools and real-time data.
4. **Research and Analysis**: Comprehensive research tools, including stock screeners, analyst reports, [market](../m/market.md) news, and [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md).
5. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including webinars, tutorials, articles, and [market](../m/market.md) analysis to help traders improve their skills.
6. **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md)**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
7. **Account Types**: Various account types including RRSPs, TFSAs, [margin](../m/margin.md) accounts, and more.
8. **Automated [Investing](../i/investing.md)**: Robo-advisory services through Questwealth Portfolios for automated, low-cost [investment management](../i/investment_management.md).
9. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile apps for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
10. **Secure and Regulated**: Questrade is regulated by top-tier financial authorities in Canada, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).

## Key Components
- **Questrade Trading**: A user-friendly [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) [offering](../o/offering.md) advanced charting, real-time data, and a [range](../r/range.md) of trading tools.
- **Questrade IQ Edge**: An advanced [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) designed for active traders, providing [robust](../r/robust.md) charting tools, [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md), and customizable layouts.
- **Mobile Trading Apps**: Mobile applications for iOS and Android with full trading functionality.
- **Research and Analysis Tools**: Comprehensive tools for [market research](../m/market_research.md), [stock analysis](../s/stock_analysis.md), and investment insights.
- **Educational Resources**: A library of educational content, including webinars, tutorials, and articles.
- **Questwealth Portfolios**: Automated [investment management](../i/investment_management.md) service using diversified portfolios of ETFs.
- **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md) Tools**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.

## Use Cases
- **Individual Investors**: Utilized by individual investors for managing their investments, accessing research, and trading in various markets.
