# Questrade

## Overview
Questrade is a leading online brokerage in Canada, known for its low-cost trading, comprehensive trading platforms, and wide range of investment products. The platform caters to individual investors and traders, offering services in stocks, ETFs, options, mutual funds, and more. Questrade is committed to providing innovative technology, valuable educational resources, and exceptional customer service.

## Features
1. **Low-Cost Trading**: Competitive pricing with low commissions and transparent fee structures.
2. **Wide Range of Instruments**: Access to stocks, ETFs, options, mutual funds, GICs, and bonds.
3. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers multiple trading platforms including Questrade Trading, Questrade IQ Edge, and mobile apps with advanced charting tools and real-time data.
4. **Research and Analysis**: Comprehensive research tools, including stock screeners, analyst reports, market news, and [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md).
5. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including webinars, tutorials, articles, and market analysis to help traders improve their skills.
6. **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md)**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.
7. **Account Types**: Various account types including RRSPs, TFSAs, margin accounts, and more.
8. **Automated Investing**: Robo-advisory services through Questwealth Portfolios for automated, low-cost investment management.
9. **Mobile Trading**: Mobile apps for iOS and Android devices, allowing trading on the go.
10. **Secure and Regulated**: Questrade is regulated by top-tier financial authorities in Canada, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).

## Key Components
- **Questrade Trading**: A user-friendly trading platform offering advanced charting, real-time data, and a range of trading tools.
- **Questrade IQ Edge**: An advanced trading platform designed for active traders, providing robust charting tools, [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md), and customizable layouts.
- **Mobile Trading Apps**: Mobile applications for iOS and Android with full trading functionality.
- **Research and Analysis Tools**: Comprehensive tools for market research, stock analysis, and investment insights.
- **Educational Resources**: A library of educational content, including webinars, tutorials, and articles.
- **Questwealth Portfolios**: Automated investment management service using diversified portfolios of ETFs.
- **[Portfolio Management](../p/portfolio_management.md) Tools**: Tools for tracking and managing investment portfolios efficiently.

## Use Cases
- **Individual Investors**: Utilized by individual investors for managing their investments, accessing research, and trading in various markets.
- **Active Traders**: Provides active traders with powerful trading tools, [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md), and advanced analysis.
- **Robo-Advisor Clients**: Offers automated investment management through Questwealth Portfolios for hands-off investors.
- **Educational Purposes**: Supports new and experienced traders with extensive educational resources and market analysis.
