# Polygon.io

## Overview
Polygon.io is a financial [market](../m/market.md) data platform that offers real-time and historical data for [stocks](../s/stock.md), [options](../o/options.md), forex, and cryptocurrencies. Known for its high-performance APIs, Polygon.io provides developers, traders, and financial institutions with reliable and scalable data solutions to power [trading algorithms](../t/trading_algorithms.md), financial applications, and research projects.

## Features
1. **[Real-Time Market Data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Provides fast and accurate real-time data for equities, [options](../o/options.md), forex, and cryptocurrencies.
2. **Historical Data**: Extensive historical data for [backtesting](../b/backtesting.md), research, and analysis, including [tick](../t/tick.md), minute, and daily data.
3. **WebSockets**: Real-time streaming data via WebSockets for low-latency applications.
4. **REST APIs**: Developer-friendly REST APIs for easy data retrieval and integration.
5. **Comprehensive Coverage**: Covers a wide [range](../r/range.md) of markets and instruments, including U.S. [stocks](../s/stock.md), forex pairs, and major cryptocurrencies.
6. **Advanced Aggregations**: Supports advanced data aggregations, including custom timeframes and data granularity.
7. **News and Events**: Access to real-time news and [market](../m/market.md)-moving events.
8. **High [Scalability](../s/scalability.md)**: Scalable [infrastructure](../i/infrastructure.md) designed to [handle](../h/handle.md) large volumes of data and high-frequency trading.
9. **Cost-Effective**: Competitive pricing plans to suit different needs and budgets, from individual developers to large enterprises.
10. **Developer Support**: Extensive documentation, SDKs, and a dedicated support team to assist with integration and usage.

## Key Components
- **[Stocks](../s/stock.md) API**: Real-time and historical data for U.S. equities, including [tick](../t/tick.md)-level data.
- **[Options](../o/options.md) API**: Comprehensive [options](../o/options.md) data, including quotes, trades, and aggregates.
- **Forex API**: Real-time and historical data for major forex pairs.
- **Crypto API**: Data for major cryptocurrencies, including real-time prices and historical data.
- **WebSocket Streams**: Real-time data streaming via WebSockets for low-latency applications.
- **REST APIs**: RESTful APIs for easy access to [market](../m/market.md) data and analytics.
- **Aggregated Data**: Advanced data [aggregation](../a/aggregation.md) tools for custom timeframes and granularity.
- **News and [Market](../m/market.md) Events**: Real-time news feeds and [market](../m/market.md) event data.

## Integrations
Polygon.io integrates seamlessly with various financial platforms, trading tools, and third-party applications to enhance its functionality. Some notable integrations include:

- **Trading Platforms**: Compatibility with popular trading platforms such as MetaTrader, [NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md), and [TradingView](../t/tradingview.md).
- **[Financial Analysis](../f/financial_analysis.md) Tools**: Integration with tools like Microsoft Excel, MATLAB, and R for advanced analysis and visualization.
- **APIs and SDKs**: Provides APIs and SDKs for custom integrations and automated data retrieval.
- **Third-Party Applications**: Support for various third-party financial applications and services.
- **Custom Applications**: API access for developing custom financial applications and [trading systems](../t/trading_systems.md).
