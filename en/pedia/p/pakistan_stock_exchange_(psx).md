# Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX)

The Pakistan Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (PSX) is the [principal](../p/principal.md) stock [exchange](../e/exchange.md) of Pakistan, and it plays a crucial role in the country's financial and economic activities. Established in 1949 as the Karachi Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (KSE), it was rebranded as PSX after the [merger](../m/merger.md) of KSE, Lahore Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (LSE), and Islamabad Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (ISE) in 2016. PSX operates under the regulatory oversight of the Securities and [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) [Commission](../c/commission.md) of Pakistan (SECP).

## Overview

PSX offers a platform for trading [shares](../s/shares.md), bonds, and other securities, and it is pivotal in mobilizing [capital](../c/capital.md) for fostering [economic growth](../e/economic_growth.md). PSX provides a transparent and efficient [market](../m/market.md), ensuring a secure environment for investors. The major [index](../i/index.md) of PSX is the KSE-100, which benchmarks the performance of the top 100 companies [listed](../l/listed.md) on the [exchange](../e/exchange.md).

## History

The evolution of the Pakistan Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) dates back to 1949:

- **1949:** Karachi Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (KSE) is established.
- **1970:** Lahore Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (LSE) is established.
- **1989:** Islamabad Stock [Exchange](../e/exchange.md) (ISE) is established.
- **2016:** [Merger](../m/merger.md) of KSE, LSE, and ISE to form PSX.

## Structure and Governance

PSX is governed by a Board of Directors, which includes representatives from the [broker](../b/broker.md) community, independent directors, and SECP nominees. The governance structure ensures that PSX operates with high standards of [corporate governance](../c/corporate_governance.md) and regulatory compliance.

## Trading Mechanism

### Market Segments

PSX operates various [market](../m/market.md) segments, which include:

- **Ready [Market](../m/market.md):** This is the main [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) where [shares](../s/shares.md) are bought and sold on a T+2 settlement [basis](../b/basis.md).
- **[Futures Market](../f/futures_market.md):** Here, [futures contracts](../f/futures_contracts.md) based on individual [stocks](../s/stock.md) and indices are traded. [Futures contracts](../f/futures_contracts.md) have specific expiry dates.
- **[Debt](../d/debt.md) [Market](../m/market.md):** It facilitates the trading of bonds and other [debt](../d/debt.md) instruments.
- **SME Board:** A platform for trading securities of small and medium enterprises.

### Trading Hours

PSX operates Monday through Friday with specific trading sessions:

- **Pre-Opening Session:** 09:15 am to 09:30 am PKT
- **Regular [Trading Session](../t/trading_session.md):** 09:30 am to 03:30 pm PKT
- **Post-Closing Session:** 03:31 pm to 04:00 pm PKT

### Order Types

PSX supports various [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md):

- **[Market Order](../m/market_order.md):** An [order](../o/order.md) to buy or sell at the best available price.
- **[Limit Order](../l/limit_order.md):** An [order](../o/order.md) to buy or sell at a specified price or better.
- **[Stop Order](../s/stop_order.md):** An [order](../o/order.md) to buy or sell once the price reaches a specified threshold.
- **GTC [Order](../o/order.md):** Good-Till-Cancelled orders remain active until executed or cancelled.

## Listing Requirements

Companies seeking to list on PSX must meet certain criteria, which include financial benchmarks, [corporate governance](../c/corporate_governance.md) standards, and [disclosure](../d/disclosure.md) requirements. The listing process involves:

1. Application for Listing
2. Approval from PSX
3. Initial Public [Offering](../o/offering.md) (IPO)
4. Commencement of Trading

## Technology Infrastructure

PSX leverages advanced technology to facilitate trading and settlement:

- **Trading System:** PSX uses the Karachi Automated Trading System (KATS).
- **[Clearing and Settlement](../c/clearing_and_settlement.md):** Managed by the National [Clearing](../c/clearing.md) Company of Pakistan Limited (NCCPL).
- **[Depository](../d/depository.md) Function:** Managed by the Central [Depository](../d/depository.md) Company of Pakistan Limited (CDC).

## Regulatory Framework

PSX operates under the jurisdiction of the SECP, which ensures adherence to regulations and standards. The regulatory framework encompasses:

- **[Market](../m/market.md) Surveillance:** Continuous monitoring of trading activities to detect irregularities.
- **[Corporate Governance](../c/corporate_governance.md):** Ensuring [listed](../l/listed.md) companies comply with governance codes.
- **[Investor](../i/investor.md) Protection:** Mechanisms to protect the rights and interests of investors.

## Key Indexes

PSX tracks several indexes, with KSE-100 being the most prominent:

- **KSE-100 [Index](../i/index.md):** Tracks the performance of the top 100 companies based on [market capitalization](../m/market_capitalization.md).
- **KSE-30 [Index](../i/index.md):** Tracks the performance of the top 30 companies.
- **All [Shares](../s/shares.md) [Index](../i/index.md):** Includes all [listed](../l/listed.md) companies on PSX.
- **Sectoral Indexes:** Tracks performance by [industry](../i/industry.md) sectors (e.g., Oil & Gas, Banks).

## Algorithmic Trading

[Algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md) in PSX involves the use of automated systems to execute trades based on predetermined criteria. Participants utilize algorithms to:

- **Analyze [Market](../m/market.md) Data:** Using historical and real-time data to identify patterns.
- **Execute Trades:** Automatically place orders based on algorithmic strategies.
- **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md):** Implement stop-loss and take-[profit](../p/profit.md) measures.

### Common Algorithmic Strategies

1. **[Trend Following](../t/trend_following.md):** Algorithms detect [market](../m/market.md) trends and execute trades aligned with those trends.
2. **[Arbitrage](../a/arbitrage.md):** Exploiting price differences between markets or instruments.
3. **[Mean Reversion](../m/mean_reversion.md):** Trading based on the assumption that prices [will](../w/will.md) revert to their historical mean.

### Regulations and Compliance

To ensure fair and orderly markets, PSX and SECP have laid down rules for [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md), including:

- **Registration and Approval:** Firms must obtain approvals before deploying algorithms.
- **[Risk](../r/risk.md) Controls:** Implementation of [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) controls, such as [order](../o/order.md) throttles.
- **Surveillance:** Continuous monitoring to prevent [market](../m/market.md) abuse.
