# Paribu

Paribu is one of the leading cryptocurrency exchanges based in Turkey. Founded in 2017, Paribu quickly made its mark in the Turkish cryptocurrency landscape by [offering](../o/offering.md) user-friendly access to a wide [range](../r/range.md) of cryptocurrencies. The platform is renowned for its high [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), extensive list of supported cryptocurrencies, [robust](../r/robust.md) [security](../s/security.md) measures, and [customer](../c/customer.md)-focused services.

## Key Features

### User-Friendly Interface
Paribu aims to provide an easy-to-navigate user experience, making it accessible for both beginners and seasoned traders. The platform's design is intuitive, ensuring that users can execute trades, [check](../c/check.md) their balances, and find information quickly and efficiently.

### Extensive Cryptocurrency Listings
Paribu supports a wide [range](../r/range.md) of cryptocurrencies, from prominent ones like [Bitcoin](../b/bitcoin.md) (BTC), [Ethereum](../e/ethereum_.md) (ETH), and [Ripple](../r/ripple.md) (XRP) to numerous altcoins. The platform regularly updates its listings based on emerging [market](../m/market.md) trends and user [demand](../d/demand.md).

### High Liquidity
One of Paribu's standout features is its high [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), which allows for faster [order](../o/order.md) [execution](../e/execution.md) and stable pricing. High [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) is beneficial for both buyers and sellers, ensuring that trades can be made quickly without significant [market](../m/market.md) distortions.

### Robust Security Measures
[Security](../s/security.md) is paramount for Paribu. The platform employs advanced [security](../s/security.md) protocols, including two-[factor](../f/factor.md) authentication (2FA), encrypted data storage, and cold storage for the majority of its cryptocurrency reserves. Regular [security](../s/security.md) audits are also conducted to ensure the platform remains safe against potential threats.

### Fiat Currency Support
Paribu supports the Turkish Lira (TRY), making it convenient for Turkish users to [deposit](../d/deposit.md) and withdraw funds using their local [currency](../c/currency.md). The easy conversion between TRY and cryptocurrencies simplifies the trading process for Turkish customers.

### Mobile Application
To cater to users who prefer trading on-the-go, Paribu offers a fully functional mobile application available on both iOS and Android platforms. The app replicates the functionalities of the web interface, ensuring a seamless trading experience across devices.
