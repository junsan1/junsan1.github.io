# Phemex

Phemex is a comprehensive cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) and [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) founded in 2019 by a group of former Morgan Stanley executives, led by Jack Tao. The platform is designed to cater to both novice and professional traders by providing a [robust](../r/robust.md) and user-friendly trading experience, combined with advanced financial tools and features.

Phemex aims to bridge the gap between traditional [finance](../f/finance.md) and the burgeoning cryptocurrency [market](../m/market.md), [offering](../o/offering.md) a [range](../r/range.md) of services including spot trading, contract trading, and a variety of [derivatives](../d/derivatives.md). The platform has rapidly gained popularity due to its competitive [fee](../f/fee.md) structure, high [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), and commitment to [security](../s/security.md).

For additional information, you can visit the official website: [Phemex](https://phemex.com).

## History and Founders

Phemex was founded in 2019 and is based in Singapore. The [leadership](../l/leadership.md) team is composed of experienced professionals from the [finance](../f/finance.md) and technology sectors. Jack Tao, the current CEO, has over a decade of experience at Morgan Stanley, focusing on electronic and [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md). The founding team believed there was a significant gap in the [market](../m/market.md) for a professional-grade cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) that could [offer](../o/offer.md) the same level of [trust](../t/trust.md), [security](../s/security.md), and performance as traditional financial platforms.

## Features and Services

### 1. **Spot Trading**

Phemex offers a [robust](../r/robust.md) spot [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) that supports a wide [range](../r/range.md) of cryptocurrencies. The platform is designed to be user-friendly, with an intuitive interface that makes it easy to execute trades. Some key features include:
- **High [Liquidity](../l/liquidity.md)**: Ensures that trades can be executed quickly and at competitive prices.
- **[Multiple](../m/multiple.md) Trading Pairs**: Offers a variety of trading pairs including BTC/USDT, ETH/USDT, and more.
- **Advanced Charting Tools**: Provides traders with [technical analysis tools](../t/technical_analysis_tools.md) to make informed decisions.

### 2. **Contract Trading**

One of Phemexâ€™s standout features is its contract trading capabilities. The platform offers perpetual contracts that allow traders to speculate on the price of an [asset](../a/asset.md) without owning it. Key features include:
- **[Leverage](../l/leverage.md)**: Traders can use [leverage](../l/leverage.md) up to 100x, allowing for potentially higher returns (but also higher risks).
- **USD-M Settled Contracts**: Contracts are settled in USD, making it easy for international traders.
- **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md) Tools**: Includes features such as stop-loss and take-[profit](../p/profit.md) orders to help manage [risk](../r/risk.md).

### 3. **Sub-Account Management**

Phemex allows users to create [multiple](../m/multiple.md) sub-accounts under a single main account. This is particularly useful for institutional traders or those managing [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md). Sub-accounts can be used to segregate funds, manage [risk](../r/risk.md), and execute different [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) simultaneously.

### 4. **Trading Competitions**

To engage its community and promote trading activity, Phemex often hosts trading competitions. These competitions [offer](../o/offer.md) substantial rewards and can be a fun way for traders to test their skills against others.

## Security Measures

Phemex takes [security](../s/security.md) very seriously and employs a [range](../r/range.md) of measures to protect user funds and data. Some of these measures include:
- **Cold Wallet Storage**: The majority of user funds are stored in cold wallets, which are not connected to the internet and are therefore less susceptible to hacking.
- **Two-[Factor](../f/factor.md) Authentication (2FA)**: Users are encouraged to enable 2FA to provide an additional layer of [security](../s/security.md) for their accounts.
- **Regular Audits**: The platform undergoes regular [security](../s/security.md) audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

## Fee Structure

Phemex offers a competitive [fee](../f/fee.md) structure designed to attract both retail and institutional traders. Fees are categorized based on the type of trading:
- **Spot Trading Fees**: Typically around 0.1% per [transaction](../t/transaction.md).
- **Contract Trading Fees**: Maker fees are around 0.01% and taker fees are around 0.06%.
- **[Withdrawal](../w/withdrawal.md) Fees**: These vary depending on the cryptocurrency but are generally low compared to [industry](../i/industry.md) standards.

## API for Algorithmic Trading

Phemex offers a comprehensive API (Application Programming Interface) that allows developers to integrate the platform's trading features into their own applications. The API supports both REST and WebSocket protocols, providing [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [options](../o/options.md) for real-time data fetching and automated trading.

### REST API

The REST API of Phemex allows users to:
- **Access [Market](../m/market.md) Data**: Fetch the latest [market](../m/market.md) data including price, [volume](../v/volume.md), and [order book](../o/order_book.md) information.
- **Execute Trades**: Place, modify, and cancel orders programmatically.
- **Manage Accounts**: View account balances, [transaction](../t/transaction.md) history, and manage sub-accounts.

### WebSocket API

The WebSocket API is designed for real-time data streaming, which is crucial for high-frequency trading and applications that require up-to-the-second information. Key features include:
- **[Real-Time Market Data](../r/real-time_market_data.md)**: Stream [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md) including ticker updates, [order book](../o/order_book.md) changes, and [trade](../t/trade.md) executions.
- **[Order](../o/order.md) Updates**: Receive real-time updates on the status of orders, including fills and cancellations.
