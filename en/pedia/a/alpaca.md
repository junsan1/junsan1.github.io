# Alpaca

## Overview
Alpaca is a [commission](../c/commission.md)-free, API-first stock brokerage platform that allows developers to build and deploy [trading algorithms](../t/trading_algorithms.md), applications, and [brokerage services](../b/brokerage_services.md). Known for its [robust](../r/robust.md) and user-friendly API, Alpaca caters to developers, algo traders, and financial institutions by providing seamless access to [real-time market data](../r/real-time_market_data.md) and trading functionalities.

## Features
1. **[Commission](../c/commission.md)-Free Trading**: Allows [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading of U.S. [stocks](../s/stock.md) and ETFs.
2. **Powerful API**: Provides a RESTful API and WebSocket for accessing real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data, as well as executing trades.
3. **Fractional [Shares](../s/shares.md)**: Supports trading of fractional [shares](../s/shares.md), enabling investment in high-priced [stocks](../s/stock.md) with smaller amounts of [capital](../c/capital.md).
4. **Paper Trading**: Offers a paper [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md) to test and refine [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) without risking real [money](../m/money.md).
5. **Advanced [Order Types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md)**: Supports various [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md) including [market](../m/market.md), limit, stop, and [trailing stop](../t/trailing_stop.md) orders.
6. **Algo Trading**: Enables [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) with support for automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).
7. **[Market](../m/market.md) Data**: Provides real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data through APIs.
8. **[Commission](../c/commission.md)-Free**: Zero [commission](../c/commission.md) fees for trading U.S. equities.
9. **Developer-Friendly**: Comprehensive documentation, SDKs, and libraries for easy integration and development.
10. **Community and Support**: Access to a community of developers and extensive support resources.

## Key Components
- **Alpaca API**: RESTful API and WebSocket for real-time and historical data access, as well as [trade](../t/trade.md) [execution](../e/execution.md).
- **Paper Trading**: [Simulated trading](../s/simulated_trading.md) environment to test strategies without [financial risk](../f/financial_risk.md).
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Data**: Access to real-time and historical data for U.S. [stocks](../s/stock.md) and ETFs.
- **Fractional [Shares](../s/shares.md)**: Ability to [trade](../t/trade.md) fractional [shares](../s/shares.md) for more flexible investment [options](../o/options.md).
- **[Commission](../c/commission.md)-Free Trading**: [Trade](../t/trade.md) U.S. [stocks](../s/stock.md) and ETFs without paying commissions.
- **Developer Resources**: Comprehensive documentation, SDKs, and support for [multiple](../m/multiple.md) programming languages.

## Integrations
Alpaca integrates with a variety of financial platforms, data providers, and third-party applications to enhance its functionality. Some notable integrations include:

- **[TradingView](../t/tradingview.md)**: Integration with [TradingView](../t/tradingview.md) for advanced charting and [trade](../t/trade.md) [execution](../e/execution.md).
- **Zapier**: Connects Alpaca with various apps to automate workflows.
- **Python and JavaScript SDKs**: Provides SDKs for Python and JavaScript for easy development.
- **Brokerage as a Service (BaaS)**: Allows other fintech companies to integrate Alpaca's [brokerage services](../b/brokerage_services.md) into their own applications.
- **Data Providers**: Integrates with [market](../m/market.md) data providers for real-time and historical data access.

## Community and Support
Alpaca offers extensive support through detailed documentation, user guides, tutorials, and a dedicated support team. Users can also access a [range](../r/range.md) of educational resources, including webinars, case studies, and community forums to share insights, ask questions, and receive assistance from other developers and Alpaca experts.

## Use Cases
- **Algorithmic Traders**: Utilized by algo traders to develop, test, and deploy automated [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) using Alpaca’s API.
- **Developers**: Supports developers in building custom trading applications and tools with [robust](../r/robust.md) APIs and SDKs.
- **Individual Investors**: Provides individual investors with [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading and access to fractional [shares](../s/shares.md).
- **Fintech Companies**: Enables fintech companies to integrate [brokerage services](../b/brokerage_services.md) into their own platforms through Alpaca’s BaaS [offering](../o/offering.md).
- **Educational Institutions**: Used by educational institutions for teaching [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) and financial application development.

Alpaca’s powerful API, [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading, and [robust](../r/robust.md) support make it an ideal choice for developers, algorithmic traders, and fintech companies seeking a flexible and scalable [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md).
