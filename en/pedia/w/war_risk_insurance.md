# War Risk Insurance

War [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) is a specialized [insurance](../i/insurance.md) product designed to protect against losses associated with war and related perils. This type of [insurance](../i/insurance.md) is primarily utilized by businesses and individuals involved in international [trade](../t/trade.md), shipping, aviation, and other industries where there's a significant [risk](../r/risk.md) of losses due to geopolitical instability and military conflicts.

## What is War Risk Insurance?

War [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) provides coverage for damages, losses, and liabilities that occur as a direct result of war, insurrections, rebellions, and other international hostilities. Conventional [insurance](../i/insurance.md) policies typically exclude such risks due to their high and unpredictable nature, making war [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) a crucial add-on for entities operating in volatile regions.

## Types of War Risk Insurance

War [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) comes in various forms depending on the [industry](../i/industry.md) and specific needs:

1. **Marine War [Risk](../r/risk.md) [Insurance](../i/insurance.md):** Covers losses or damages to ships, cargo, and other marine assets resulting from war-related activities. This can include coverage for hull damage, cargo loss, and financial losses due to delays.

2. **Aviation War [Risk](../r/risk.md) [Insurance](../i/insurance.md):** Offers protection for aircraft and aviation operations against war-related incidents. This includes damage to aircraft, [liability](../l/liability.md) for passenger injuries, and disruptions to airline operations.

3. **Land-Based War [Risk](../r/risk.md) [Insurance](../i/insurance.md):** Suitable for businesses and properties located in conflict-prone areas. It covers physical damage to buildings, equipment, and other assets due to war activities.

## Coverage Inclusions and Exclusions

War [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) policies are highly customizable but generally include coverage for:
- Direct physical damage or destruction caused by acts of war.
- Financial losses due to disruptions in operations or [trade](../t/trade.md).
- [Liability](../l/liability.md) claims arising from war-related incidents.
- Loss or damage to cargo in transit within war zones.

Exclusions typically consist of:
- Losses due to internal political unrest not classified as war.
- Non-war-related financial losses.
- Damage due to recaptured territories where control has been re-established.
- Nuclear, biological, or chemical attacks might be excluded depending on the policy.

## How War Risk Insurance Works

### Risk Assessment and Premium Calculation

[Insurance](../i/insurance.md) providers perform a thorough assessment of the potential risks involved before issuing war [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md). The [premium](../p/premium.md) calculation is based on factors such as:
- Geopolitical stability of the regions involved.
- Historical incidence of war or conflict in those regions.
- Type and [value](../v/value.md) of assets being insured.
- [Duration](../d/duration.md) and [scope](../s/scope.md) of coverage required.

### Policy Issuance and Claims Process

Once a policy is issued, the insured entity is protected according to the terms specified. The claims process involves:
- Notification of the incident to the insurer immediately or as specified in the policy.
- Submission of necessary documentation and evidence to substantiate the claim.
- Assessment by the insurer or designated adjusters to determine the validity and extent of the claim.
- Settlement of the claim as per the terms of the policy.

For shipping companies that operate in areas like the Middle East or the Horn of Africa, war [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) is indispensable. ### Aviation Industry

Airlines operating in conflict zones must have aviation war [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) to cover potential losses. [Lloyd's of London](https://www.lloyds.com/) is one of the key [market](../m/market.md) leaders in providing war [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md) for aviation.

### Multinational Corporations

Corporations with assets in unstable regions, like oil and gas companies operating in the Middle East and Africa, often require comprehensive war [risk](../r/risk.md) [insurance](../i/insurance.md). Companies like [ExxonMobil](https://corporate.exxonmobil.com/) ensure their investments and operations in these areas are safeguarded through extensive war [risk](../r/risk.md) coverage.
