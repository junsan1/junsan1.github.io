# WhiteBIT

WhiteBIT is a cryptocurrency [exchange](../e/exchange.md) platform that allows users to buy, sell, and [trade](../t/trade.md) a variety of digital assets. Launching in 2018, WhiteBIT has grown significantly and now holds a substantial presence in the cryptocurrency [market](../m/market.md). The platform is known for its user-friendly interface, high [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), low trading fees, and state-of-the-art [security](../s/security.md) measures. Available for users from all around the globe, WhiteBIT aims to provide a secure, efficient, and seamless trading experience for both novice and experienced traders.

## Overview

WhiteBIT offers a variety of services aimed at facilitating cryptocurrency trading. These include spot trading, [futures](../f/futures.md) trading, [margin trading](../m/margin_trading.md), and various earning programs. With over 300 trading pairs and a [robust](../r/robust.md) API, WhiteBIT ensures a wide [range](../r/range.md) of [options](../o/options.md) for traders. The platform supports both fiat and cryptocurrency deposits, making it versatile and accessible.

## Earning Programs

WhiteBIT offers [multiple](../m/multiple.md) earning programs for its users. These include staking, lending, and various promotional campaigns. Users can earn rewards by staking their assets on the platform or by participating in lending programs where they can [loan](../l/loan.md) their cryptocurrency for [interest](../i/interest.md).

### Staking

Staking involves locking up your assets for a certain period in [return](../r/return.md) for rewards. WhiteBIT offers a variety of staking [options](../o/options.md), ranging from flexible to fixed terms, providing users with [multiple](../m/multiple.md) ways to earn passively.

### Lending

Lending programs allow users to earn [interest](../i/interest.md) on their crypto assets by lending them out to other traders. The [interest](../i/interest.md) rates are competitive, providing an additional stream of [income](../i/income.md) for users.

## Security Measures

WhiteBIT employs a multi-layered approach to [security](../s/security.md). While 96% of assets are stored in cold wallets, the platform also uses encrypted SSL certificates, anti-phishing measures, and continuous monitoring to detect unusual activities. The two-[factor](../f/factor.md) authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of [security](../s/security.md) to user accounts.

### Cold and Hot Wallets

Cold wallets are offline storage that significantly reduces the [risk](../r/risk.md) of hacks. Typically, only 4% of assets are kept in hot wallets, which are connected to the internet and used for day-to-[day trading](../d/day_trading.md) activities.

### Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

The use of 2FA adds an extra layer of [security](../s/security.md) by requiring users to provide two forms of identification before accessing their accounts.

## API and Algorithmic Trading

WhiteBIT's API is designed to cater to traders who prefer [algorithmic trading](../a/accountability.md). The API supports RESTful methods, as well as WebSocket for real-time data and [trade](../t/trade.md) executions. This makes it suitable for high-frequency trading (HFT) as well as more complex [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md).

### REST API

The REST API allows users to perform various actions such as retrieving account information, executing trades, and accessing [market](../m/market.md) data. The API endpoint supports a variety of HTTP methods, including GET, POST, and DELETE.

### WebSocket API

The WebSocket API offers real-time data streaming, which is essential for algorithmic traders. It provides real-time updates on [market](../m/market.md) data, [order book](../o/order_book.md) changes, and [trade](../t/trade.md) completions.
