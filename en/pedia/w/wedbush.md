# Wedbush

## Overview
Wedbush Securities is a leading financial services and investment [firm](../f/firm.md) based in Los Angeles, California. Established in 1955, Wedbush offers a comprehensive [range](../r/range.md) of services including brokerage, [wealth management](../w/wealth_management.md), [investment banking](../i/investment_banking.md), research, and trading. The [firm](../f/firm.md) caters to individual investors, institutional clients, and corporations, providing personalized financial solutions and advanced trading capabilities.

## Key Features
1. **[Brokerage Services](../b/brokerage_services.md)**: Wedbush provides full-service [brokerage services](../b/brokerage_services.md), [offering](../o/offering.md) access to a wide [range](../r/range.md) of investment products including [stocks](../s/stock.md), bonds, mutual funds, ETFs, and [options](../o/options.md).

2. **[Wealth Management](../w/wealth_management.md)**: The [firm](../f/firm.md) offers personalized [wealth management](../w/wealth_management.md) services, including [financial planning](../f/financial_planning.md), [retirement planning](../r/retirement_planning.md), [estate planning](../e/estate_planning.md), and [portfolio management](../p/portfolio_management.md), tailored to meet the unique needs of individual clients.

3. **[Investment Banking](../i/investment_banking.md)**: Wedbush’s [investment banking](../i/investment_banking.md) division provides advisory services for mergers and acquisitions, [capital](../c/capital.md) raising, and [corporate finance](../c/corporate_finance.md), serving middle-[market](../m/market.md) companies across various industries.

4. **Research**: Wedbush provides in-depth [equity research](../e/equity_research.md) and [market](../m/market.md) analysis, covering a wide [range](../r/range.md) of sectors and companies. Their research team offers valuable insights to help clients make informed investment decisions.

5. **Trading Services**: Wedbush offers advanced trading services, including equities, [options](../o/options.md), and [fixed income](../f/fixed_income.md) trading. The [firm](../f/firm.md) provides direct [market](../m/market.md) access (DMA) and [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) solutions for institutional clients.

6. **[Clearing](../c/clearing.md) Services**: As a self-[clearing](../c/clearing.md) [firm](../f/firm.md), Wedbush provides [clearing and settlement](../c/clearing_and_settlement.md) services for introducing brokers and other financial institutions, ensuring efficient and secure [transaction](../t/transaction.md) processing.

7. **Technology and Tools**: Wedbush provides clients with access to advanced trading platforms, [market](../m/market.md) data, and analytical tools to enhance their trading and investment activities.

8. **Corporate Services**: Wedbush offers corporate services such as stock plan administration, corporate [cash management](../c/cash_management.md), and executive services, helping businesses manage their financial needs.
