# W. P. Carey School of Business

The W. P. Carey School of [Business](../b/business.md) at Arizona State University is one of the largest and most prestigious [business](../b/business.md) schools in the United States. It offers a vast array of programs and degrees designed to cater to the educational needs of various levels of students, from undergraduates to executives. This document [will](../w/will.md) delve into the diverse aspects of the W. P. Carey School of [Business](../b/business.md), including its history, programs, research initiatives, and [industry](../i/industry.md) partnerships, among other topics. 

## History

The W. P. Carey School of [Business](../b/business.md) was established in 1955 and is named after William Polk Carey, an influential [entrepreneur](../e/entrepreneur.md) and philanthropist who made significant contributions to the institution. Over the years, the school has experienced extensive growth in terms of its student body, faculty, and facilities, establishing itself as a premier institution for [business](../b/business.md) education. Its commitment to academic excellence and innovative research has attracted students and scholars from around the globe.

## Academic Programs

### Undergraduate Programs

The W. P. Carey School offers a variety of undergraduate programs designed to equip students with essential [business](../b/business.md) skills and knowledge. These include:

- **Bachelor of Science in Accountancy**
- **Bachelor of Science in [Business](../b/business.md) [Data Analytics](../d/data_analytics.md)**
- **Bachelor of Science in [Finance](../f/finance.md)**
- **Bachelor of Science in [Marketing](../m/marketing.md)**
- **Bachelor of Science in [Supply Chain](../s/supply_chain.md) Management**

Each program blends theoretical knowledge with practical applications, preparing students for a dynamic and competitive [business](../b/business.md) environment.

### Graduate Programs

For graduate students, the W. P. Carey School offers several highly-regarded programs:

- **Master of [Business](../b/business.md) Administration (MBA)**
  - Full-time MBA
  - Evening MBA
  - Online MBA
  - Executive MBA

- **Master of Science (MS)**
  - MS in [Business](../b/business.md) Analytics
  - MS in [Finance](../f/finance.md)
  - MS in Information Management
  - MS in [Supply Chain](../s/supply_chain.md) Management

- **Doctoral Programs (PhD)**
  - PhD in [Economics](../e/economics.md)
  - PhD in [Finance](../f/finance.md)
  - PhD in Management

These programs are designed to develop advanced [business](../b/business.md) acumen, [leadership](../l/leadership.md) skills, and research capabilities, enabling graduates to excel in academia and [industry](../i/industry.md).

## Faculty and Research

The W. P. Carey School boasts a distinguished faculty composed of leading experts and researchers in various fields of [business](../b/business.md). The school's commitment to research excellence is evidenced by its numerous research centers and initiatives, including:

- **The Center for Advanced [Procurement](../p/procurement.md) Strategy (CAPS) Research**
- **The Center for Services [Leadership](../l/leadership.md) (CSL)**
- **The Digital Innovation and Strategy Laboratory**
- **The Entrepreneurship and Innovation Research Group**

Faculty members actively publish in top-tier academic journals, contribute to [industry](../i/industry.md) white papers, and engage in groundbreaking research that influences both [business](../b/business.md) practices and policy.

## Industry Partnerships and Career Services

One of the strengths of the W. P. Carey School is its extensive network of [industry](../i/industry.md) partnerships. These collaborations provide students with invaluable opportunities for internships, job placements, and experiential learning. Notable partners include [Fortune 500](../f/fortune_500.md) companies and leading firms in various sectors.

The school's dedicated career services department offers comprehensive support to help students navigate their career paths. Services include career coaching, [resume](../r/resume.md) workshops, interview preparation, and [networking](../n/networking.md) events. These resources ensure that graduates are well-prepared to enter the workforce and achieve their professional goals.

## Global Reach and Diversity

The W. P. Carey School is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment. Global programs and partnerships, such as [exchange](../e/exchange.md) programs and international study tours, further expand the school's reach and impact.
