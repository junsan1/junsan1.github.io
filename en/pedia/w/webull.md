# Webull

## Overview
Webull is a leading online brokerage platform [offering](../o/offering.md) [commission](../c/commission.md)-free trading services across a wide [range](../r/range.md) of financial instruments, including [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), and cryptocurrencies. Known for its advanced trading technology, comprehensive [market](../m/market.md) data, and user-friendly mobile and web platforms, Webull caters to both individual investors and active traders.

## Features
1. **[Commission](../c/commission.md)-Free Trading**: [Trade](../t/trade.md) [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), and cryptocurrencies with no [commission](../c/commission.md) fees.
2. **Wide [Range](../r/range.md) of Instruments**: Access to a variety of financial instruments, including [stocks](../s/stock.md), ETFs, [options](../o/options.md), and cryptocurrencies.
3. **Advanced Trading Platforms**: Offers [multiple](../m/multiple.md) trading platforms, including Webull Desktop, Webull Web, and Webull Mobile with advanced charting tools and real-time data.
4. **Comprehensive [Market](../m/market.md) Data**: Access to real-time quotes, historical data, and in-depth [market](../m/market.md) analysis.
5. **Educational Resources**: Extensive educational materials, including webinars, tutorials, articles, and [market](../m/market.md) analysis to help traders improve their skills.
6. **Paper Trading**: Practice trading with a free paper trading feature to test strategies without risking real [money](../m/money.md).
7. **[Technical Indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md)**: Provides a wide [range](../r/range.md) of [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md) and charting tools for in-depth [market](../m/market.md) analysis.
8. **Customizable Alerts**: Set custom alerts for price movements, news updates, and [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md).
9. **Research and Analysis**: Access to comprehensive research reports, [market](../m/market.md) news, and advanced analysis tools.
10. **Secure and Regulated**: Webull is regulated by top-tier financial authorities, ensuring a secure [trading environment](../t/trading_environment.md).

## Key Components
- **Webull Desktop**: An advanced [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) [offering](../o/offering.md) [robust](../r/robust.md) charting tools, [technical analysis](../t/technical_analysis.md), and customizable layouts.
- **Webull Web**: A web-based platform that provides a similar trading experience as the desktop platform, with easy access to account information and trading tools.
- **Webull Mobile App**: Mobile application for trading, account management, and [market research](../m/market_research.md) on the go.
- **Paper Trading**: A feature that allows users to practice [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) without using real [money](../m/money.md).
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Data and Research**: Comprehensive tools for [market research](../m/market_research.md), [stock analysis](../s/stock_analysis.md), and investment insights.
- **Educational Resources**: A library of educational content, including webinars, tutorials, and articles.
- **Customizable Alerts**: Tools for setting custom alerts based on price movements, news updates, and [technical indicators](../t/technical_indicators.md).
