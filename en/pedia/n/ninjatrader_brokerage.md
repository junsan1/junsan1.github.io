# NinjaTrader Brokerage

[NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md) Brokerage is a leading provider of trading technology and [brokerage services](../b/brokerage_services.md) for active traders. Specializing in [futures](../f/futures.md) and forex trading, [NinjaTrader](../n/ninjatrader.md) offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed to support trading, charting, and [market](../m/market.md) analysis.
