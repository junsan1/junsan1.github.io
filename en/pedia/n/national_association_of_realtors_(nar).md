# National Association of Realtors (NAR)

The National Association of Realtors (NAR) is a significant [trade](../t/trade.md) association in the United States, representing professionals in the [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) [industry](../i/industry.md). Established in 1908, and originally named the National Association of [Real Estate](../r/real_estate.md) Exchanges, it has grown to become one of the largest [trade](../t/trade.md) associations in the country, with over 1.4 million members as of 2021. The members, known as Realtors, include residential and [commercial real estate](../c/commercial_real_estate.md) brokers, salespeople, property managers, appraisers, counselors, and others engaged in all aspects of the [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) [industry](../i/industry.md).

## Membership and Structure

Membership in NAR offers numerous benefits, including access to [industry](../i/industry.md) publications, educational programs, certification courses, and a network of professionals. The association is structured into three tiers: the national level (NAR), state associations, and local [Realtor](../r/realtor.md) boards or associations. Each level provides specific benefits and services tailored to its geographic and professional constituency.

## Code of Ethics

One of the defining features of NAR is its [Code of Ethics](../c/code_of_ethics.md), first adopted in 1913. The Code sets forth [obligations](../o/obligation.md) higher than those mandated by law for [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) professionals, emphasizing duties to clients, the public, and other realtors. It covers various aspects, such as:

- Pledging to protect and promote the interests of their clients.
- Avoiding misrepresentation, exaggeration, or concealment of pertinent facts.
- Cooperation with other brokers.
- Compliance with advertising regulations.

## Legislative Advocacy

NAR is deeply involved in legislative advocacy, working to influence policies that affect [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) professionals and property owners. The association has a political action committee (PAC) known as the Realtors Political Action Committee (RPAC), which supports candidates who champion [real estate](../r/real_estate.md)-related issues. NAR's advocacy efforts are directed at various areas, including tax policies, housing [finance](../f/finance.md) reform, environmental regulations, and fair housing laws.

## Research and Data

NAR provides extensive [market research](../m/market_research.md) and economic data to its members and the public. This includes:

- Monthly reports such as the Existing-[Home](../h/home.md) Sales report.
- Annual surveys like the Profile of [Home](../h/home.md) Buyers and Sellers.
- The REALTORS Confidence [Index](../i/index.md).
- [Commercial real estate](../c/commercial_real_estate.md) [market](../m/market.md) reports.

These resources help [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) professionals make informed decisions based on current [market](../m/market.md) conditions and trends.

## Education and Professional Development

Continuing education is a cornerstone of NAR's offerings. The association provides numerous educational resources through its various institutes, societies, and councils. Some prominent programs and certifications include:

- The Graduate, [Realtor](../r/realtor.md) Institute (GRI).
- The Certified Residential Specialist (CRS).
- The Accredited Buyer's Representative (ABR).
- The Seniors [Real Estate](../r/real_estate.md) Specialist (SRES).

These programs ensure that [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) professionals can enhance their skills, stay current with [industry](../i/industry.md) developments, and better serve their clients.

## Technology and Innovation

NAR is committed to embracing technology and innovation in the [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) [industry](../i/industry.md). It supports initiatives that enable members to utilize the latest tools and platforms to serve their clients more effectively. The Realtors Property Resource (RPR) is an example of this commitment, providing members with comprehensive data on over 160 million properties across the U.S. Additionally, NAR's Second Century Ventures (SCV) is an [investment fund](../i/investment_fund.md) that supports the growth of innovative companies to benefit [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) professionals.

## Global Outreach

While NAR is primarily focused on the United States, it has a significant international presence, with members in over 70 countries. The association seeks to foster global partnerships and promote international [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) [business](../b/business.md) opportunities for its members. Through events like the International [Real Estate](../r/real_estate.md) Conference and alliances with [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) organizations worldwide, NAR provides its members with a platform to expand their international [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) [business](../b/business.md).

## Conclusion

The National Association of Realtors (NAR) plays a critical role in the [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) [industry](../i/industry.md), providing advocacy, education, [ethical standards](../e/ethical_standards_in_trading.md), and resources to its members. It supports [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) professionals through various initiatives, ensuring they can achieve success and serve their clients effectively. From legislative advocacy to promoting ethical practices, NAR's influence extends across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) aspects of the [real estate](../r/real_estate.md) [market](../m/market.md), both domestically and globally.
