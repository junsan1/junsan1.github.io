# Oxford Saïd Business School (SBS)

Oxford Saïd [Business](../b/business.md) School, often referred to as Saïd [Business](../b/business.md) School or simply SBS, is part of the University of Oxford in the United Kingdom. Founded in 1996, it is one of the youngest and most vibrant [business](../b/business.md) schools in the world. The school is named after Wafic Saïd, a prominent Saudi-Syrian businessman and philanthropist who provided the foundational [endowment](../e/endowment.md). Despite its relatively young age, SBS has garnered a reputation for excellence and innovation in [business](../b/business.md) education, fostering a global community of leaders, entrepreneurs, and scholars.

## History and Foundation

SBS was established with the ambition to provide a world-class [business](../b/business.md) education embedded within one of the world's most prestigious universities. The school was officially opened on November 5, 1996. Its founding was supported by a £20 million donation from Wafic Saïd, making it possible to launch the school with an initial [endowment](../e/endowment.md) and build the Saïd [Business](../b/business.md) School building.

## Academic Programs

SBS offers a diverse array of academic programs tailored to meet the needs of future leaders, entrepreneurs, and policymakers. These programs include undergraduate, graduate, and executive education courses.

### Undergraduate Programs

- **BA in [Economics](../e/economics.md) and Management**: This [joint](../j/joint.md) degree provides a solid grounding in both economic theory and [business](../b/business.md) practice, drawing on the extensive resources and expertise available at the University of Oxford.

### Graduate Programs

- **MBA (Master of [Business](../b/business.md) Administration)**: The Oxford MBA is a one-year, full-time program that combines rigorous academic challenges with a wide [range](../r/range.md) of extracurricular activities. It attracts students from all around the globe and emphasizes [leadership](../l/leadership.md), entrepreneurship, and strategic thinking.
- **EMBA (Executive Master of [Business](../b/business.md) Administration)**: The Oxford EMBA is designed for mid-career professionals looking to enhance their management skills while continuing to work. The program is delivered in modular format, allowing students to balance their studies with their professional responsibilities.
- **MFE (Master of [Financial Economics](../f/financial_economics.md))**: This is a specialist [finance](../f/finance.md) degree that is highly quantitative in nature, designed for students who intend to pursue careers in [investment banking](../i/investment_banking.md), [asset management](../a/asset_management.md), or related fields.
- **MSc in Major Programme Management**: This program is tailored for professionals managing and overseeing major projects, focusing on the skills and knowledge required to lead large-scale, complex programs.

### Doctoral Programs

- **DPhil in Management Studies**: The DPhil program is aimed at those interested in an academic career or high-level research roles. It encourages students to conduct in-depth research and contribute to the body of knowledge in fields such as [finance](../f/finance.md), [marketing](../m/marketing.md), operations, and organizational behavior.

### Executive Education

SBS also offers a variety of executive education programs that cater to both individuals and organizations. These programs are designed to enhance [leadership](../l/leadership.md) skills, provide innovative solutions to [business](../b/business.md) challenges, and inspire transformative change.

## Research and Innovation

Research at Saïd [Business](../b/business.md) School is characterized by a commitment to addressing real-world [business](../b/business.md) challenges and shaping policies. The school is [home](../h/home.md) to several research centers and initiatives:

- **Oxford University Centre for Corporate Reputation**: This center focuses on understanding how reputations are formed, sustained, and managed in the corporate world.
- **Oxford [Private Equity](../p/private_equity.md) Institute**: This institute provides insights and understanding into the [private equity](../p/private_equity.md) [industry](../i/industry.md), covering areas such as investment strategies, performance measurement, and the impact of [private equity](../p/private_equity.md) on businesses and economies.
- **Oxford Future of [Finance](../f/finance.md) and Technology Initiative**: This initiative explores the intersection of [finance](../f/finance.md) and technology, including areas like fintech, digital currencies, and [regtech](../r/regtech.md).
- **Skoll Centre for Social Entrepreneurship**: The Skoll Centre supports social entrepreneurs and promotes academic research in social innovation and entrepreneurship.

## Alumni Network

SBS boasts a [robust](../r/robust.md) and influential alumni network that spans across different industries and regions. The school's alumni are leaders in [business](../b/business.md), government, and non-[profit](../p/profit.md) organizations, and they maintain a strong connection to the school, contributing to its mission and supporting current students through mentorship and [networking](../n/networking.md) opportunities.

## Facilities and Campus

The Saïd [Business](../b/business.md) School main building is located at Park End Street, Oxford. It is an iconic structure that blends modern architectural design with the historic essence of Oxford. The building features state-of-the-art classrooms, lecture theaters, and other learning spaces designed to foster collaboration and innovation.
