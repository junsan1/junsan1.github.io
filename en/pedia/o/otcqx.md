# OTCQX

The OTCQX Best [Market](../m/market.md) is the highest tier of the [over-the-counter (OTC) markets](../o/over-the-counter_markets.md) operated by the OTC Markets Group Inc. It provides a platform for established, [investor](../i/investor.md)-focused U.S. and international companies to [trade](../t/trade.md) their securities. Companies on the OTCQX [Market](../m/market.md) must meet high financial standards, adhere to best practice [corporate governance](../c/corporate_governance.md), and demonstrate compliance with U.S. securities laws, making it an attractive choice for companies that wish to benefit from access to U.S. investors without the burdens and expenses associated with a traditional [exchange](../e/exchange.md) listing, such as those on the NYSE or [NASDAQ](../n/nasdaq.md).

## Overview

OTCQX is often perceived as a more transparent and higher-quality alternative to the lower tiers of the OTC markets, such as the OTCQB and the Pink [Open Market](../o/open_market.md). The OTCQX [market](../m/market.md) offers more credibility and [visibility](../v/visibility.md) due to its rigorous admission standards. Companies [listed](../l/listed.md) on this [market](../m/market.md) often include large multinational corporations, smaller but fundamentally sound U.S. companies, and a variety of international companies looking to list in the United States.

## Admission Criteria

To qualify for the OTCQX, companies need to meet specific financial and governance standards, including:

1. **Minimum Financial Assets:** Companies must have substantial financial assets and operating history. Specific financial criteria can vary based on the jurisdiction.
2. **Audited Financials:** Annual [financial statements](../f/financial_statements.md) must be audited by a [Public Company](../p/public_company.md) [Accounting](../a/accounting.md) Oversight Board (PCAOB) registered or equivalent internationally recognized auditor.
3. **Current [Disclosure](../d/disclosure.md):** Companies must maintain current [disclosure](../d/disclosure.md) of their financial position and material news.
4. **Sponsorship:** They must be sponsored by a qualified third-party advisor, such as an investment [bank](../b/bank.md) or law [firm](../f/firm.md), ensuring the company's credibility and [transparency](../t/transparency.md).
5. **[Corporate Governance](../c/corporate_governance.md):** Firms need to adhere to specific [corporate governance](../c/corporate_governance.md) standards, including board independence and committee structures.

## Benefits for Companies

1. **Increased [Visibility](../v/visibility.md):** Companies on the OTCQX enjoy greater [visibility](../v/visibility.md) due to a more rigorous vetting process.
2. **Enhanced [Liquidity](../l/liquidity.md):** Due to increased [investor](../i/investor.md) confidence and higher reporting standards, these companies often experience better [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md) in their [stocks](../s/stock.md).
3. **Cost-Effective:** Listing on the OTCQX is generally more cost-effective than listing on a traditional [exchange](../e/exchange.md), while still providing access to U.S. [capital markets](../c/capital_markets.md).
4. **[Premium](../p/premium.md) [Investor](../i/investor.md) Base:** The OTCQX tends to attract long-term investors due to its higher [disclosure](../d/disclosure.md) and governance standards.
5. **Compliance Simplification:** The [market](../m/market.md) offers simplified compliance requirements compared to traditional exchanges, making it easier for companies to manage reporting and governance [obligations](../o/obligation.md).

## Notable Companies on OTCQX

Many highly reputable and internationally recognized companies choose to list on the OTCQX due to its benefits and [visibility](../v/visibility.md). Some notable names include:

- **Heineken N.V. (HEINY):** The Dutch brewing giant that benefits from the improved exposure to the U.S. investment community while maintaining its primary listing on [Euronext Amsterdam](../e/euronext_amsterdam.md).
- **Roche Holding Ltd (RHHBY):** The Swiss healthcare company, which enhances its U.S. [market](../m/market.md) presence through OTCQX.
- **BASF SE (BASFY):** A major player in the chemical [industry](../i/industry.md) with primary listings in Germany, utilizing OTCQX for U.S. [investor](../i/investor.md) engagement.

## Investment Considerations

Investors looking at OTCQX-[listed](../l/listed.md) companies should consider the following points:

1. **[Due Diligence](../d/due_diligence.md):** Conduct thorough [due diligence](../d/due_diligence.md), focusing on the specific criteria that qualified the company for OTCQX.
2. **[Liquidity](../l/liquidity.md):** Although OTCQX companies generally experience better [liquidity](../l/liquidity.md), it may still be lower compared to traditional exchanges.
3. **Regulatory Environment:** Understand the [security](../s/security.md)'s primary regulatory environment and how it may affect [investor](../i/investor.md) rights and returns.
4. **[Diversification](../d/diversification.md):** OTCQX listings include a broad [range](../r/range.md) of industries and sectors, enabling [diversification](../d/diversification.md) within a portfolio.
5. **[Currency](../c/currency.md) [Risk](../r/risk.md):** For international companies, there may be additional [currency](../c/currency.md) risks involved if the company's primary operations are outside the U.S.

## Conclusion

The OTCQX Best [Market](../m/market.md) provides a reputable and cost-effective platform for companies seeking access to U.S. investors without the stringent regulations of traditional exchanges. The high standards of financial [transparency](../t/transparency.md), governance, and compliance required for OTCQX listing ensure that only quality companies can [trade](../t/trade.md) on this [market](../m/market.md). This offers a relative level of [assurance](../a/assurance.md) and attractiveness for both domestic and international investors. For those looking to explore securities within a trusted yet flexible framework, the OTCQX Best [Market](../m/market.md) presents a viable and attractive opportunity.

More information can be obtained directly on the [OTC Markets](https://www.otcmarkets.com/) official website.
