# Orc Trader

## Overview
Orc [Trader](../t/trader.md) is a sophisticated [trading platform](../t/trading_platform.md) developed by Itiviti (formerly known as Orc Group) that caters to professional traders and financial institutions. The platform is designed for high-performance trading, providing advanced tools for [market](../m/market.md) making, [arbitrage](../a/arbitrage.md), and [risk management](../r/risk_management.md). Orc [Trader](../t/trader.md) is renowned for its robustness, flexibility, and comprehensive trading functionalities.

## Features
1. **High-Performance Trading**: Optimized for low-latency, high-frequency trading across [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [asset](../a/asset.md) classes.
2. **[Market](../m/market.md) Making**: Advanced tools for [market](../m/market.md) making and [liquidity provision](../l/liquidity_provision.md).
3. **[Arbitrage](../a/arbitrage.md) Trading**: Capabilities for identifying and executing [arbitrage](../a/arbitrage.md) opportunities.
4. **[Algorithmic Trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md)**: Supports the development and deployment of complex [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) strategies.
5. **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md)**: Real-time [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) tools to monitor and control trading risks.
6. **Advanced Analytics**: Comprehensive analytics and reporting tools for performance analysis and decision-making.
7. **Customizable Interface**: Highly customizable user interface to meet specific trading requirements.
8. **Integration**: Seamless integration with various exchanges, brokers, and data providers.
9. **Multi-[Asset](../a/asset.md) Support**: Supports trading in equities, [options](../o/options.md), [futures](../f/futures.md), forex, and [fixed income](../f/fixed_income.md).
10. **[Order Management](../o/order_management_in_trading.md)**: Sophisticated [order management](../o/order_management_in_trading.md) system with [multiple](../m/multiple.md) [order types](../o/order_types_in_trading.md) and [execution](../e/execution.md) strategies.

## Key Components
- **Trading Engine**: High-performance core engine designed for low-latency [order](../o/order.md) [execution](../e/execution.md) and real-time data processing.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Making Tools**: Tools for quoting, hedging, and managing [market](../m/market.md) making operations.
- **[Algorithmic Trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) Module**: Supports the creation and [execution](../e/execution.md) of [algorithmic trading](../a/algorithmic_trading.md) strategies.
- **[Risk Management](../r/risk_management.md) Module**: Real-time [risk management](../r/risk_management.md) and monitoring tools to manage exposure and ensure compliance.
- **[Order](../o/order.md) and [Execution](../e/execution.md) Management**: Advanced [order](../o/order.md) and [execution](../e/execution.md) management system to optimize trading operations.
- **Analytics and Reporting**: Tools for generating detailed analytics and reports on [trading performance](../t/trading_performance.md) and [market](../m/market.md) conditions.
- **Custom Scripting**: Ability to create custom [trading strategies](../t/trading_strategies.md) and tools using scripting languages.

## Integrations
Orc [Trader](../t/trader.md) integrates with a wide [range](../r/range.md) of exchanges, brokers, and data providers to [offer](../o/offer.md) a seamless trading experience. Some notable integrations include:

- **Exchanges**: Direct connectivity to major global exchanges for equities, [options](../o/options.md), [futures](../f/futures.md), and forex trading.
- **Brokerage Firms**: Integration with [multiple](../m/multiple.md) brokers for seamless [order](../o/order.md) [execution](../e/execution.md) and account management.
- **[Market](../m/market.md) Data Providers**: Access to real-time and historical [market](../m/market.md) data from leading data providers.
- **Third-Party Tools**: Support for various third-party tools and plugins to extend platform functionality.
- **APIs**: APIs for custom integrations and development of [proprietary trading](../p/proprietary_trading.md) solutions.

## Community and Support
